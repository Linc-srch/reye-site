Cryptography

密码学

Research focus

研究方向

Domain: Hardware Security, Applied Cryptography, Edge Computing, Audio Processing Core Research Objective: Developing hardware-accelerated cryptographic security and real-time biometric authentication architectures for edge devices and AI Neural Processing Units (NPUs). Taxonomy of Research Areas: 1. NPU AI Security & Applied Cryptography: Securing on-device AI execution through hardware-integrated cryptographic protocols. Focus areas include: • NPU-Native Symmetric Cryptography: Hardware acceleration of lightweight symmetric-key ciphers (e.g., AES, ChaCha20) directly on NPU tensor cores, enabling low-latency, on-the-fly AI model decryption and secure execution. • Quantum-Resistant Edge Security: Evaluating and integrating Post-Quantum Cryptography (PQC) standards for long-term secure edge compute. 2. Audio Filtering Technology & Edge Processing: Advancing real-time audio stream management for smart edge devices (e.g., AI spectacles) to optimize command processing. Key technical mechanisms include: • Voice Activity Detection (VAD) & Speech Activity Detection (SAD): Intelligent endpointing algorithms to differentiate speech from silence, allowing microphones to remain active without falsely registering breathing or pauses as commands. • Dynamic Noise Suppression: Real-time filtering to isolate target audio from background environmental noise. 3. Continuous Identity Verification & Biometrics: Ensuring the physical operator of the edge device matches the authorized digital identity. Key implementations include: • Active Speaker Detection (ASD): Spatial and contextual filtering to verify that the registered audio stream originates directly from the individual wearing the device. • Voice Biometrics: Cryptographically binding the user's vocal signature to their agentic authorization permissions to prevent impersonation. 领域： 硬件安全、应用密码学、边缘计算、音频处理 核心研究目标： 开发用于边缘设备和人工智能神经网络处理器 (NPU) 的硬件加速密码安全与实时生物特征身份验证架构。 研究领域分类： 1. NPU 人工智能安全与应用密码学： 通过硬件集成的密码协议保障设备端 AI 执行的安全。重点领域包括： • NPU 原生对称密码学： 在张量核心 (tensor cores) 上直接对轻量级对称密钥密码（例如 AES, ChaCha20）进行硬件加速，以实现低延迟、实时的 AI 模型解密与安全执行。 • 抗量子边缘安全： 评估并集成后量子密码学 (PQC) 标准，以确保长期的边缘计算安全。 2. 音频过滤技术与边缘处理： 推进智能边缘设备（例如 AI 智能眼镜）的实时音频流管理，以优化命令处理。关键技术机制包括： • 语音活动检测 (VAD) 与 语音端点检测 (SAD)： 智能端点检测算法，用于区分语音和静音，使麦克风保持开启状态，而不会将呼吸或停顿误认为指令。 • 动态噪声抑制： 实时过滤，从背景环境噪声中分离出目标音频。 3. 持续身份验证与生物识别： 确保边缘设备的物理操作者与授权的数字身份相匹配。关键实现包括： • 活跃说话人检测 (ASD)： 空间和上下文过滤，以验证注册的音频流直接源自佩戴设备的个体。 声纹识别 (Voice Biometrics)： 将用户的声音特征与其代理授权权限进行密码学绑定，以防止身份冒充

Digital Identity & Trust

数字身份与信任

Research focus

研究方向

Domain: Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, Digital Identity Core Research Objective: Lifecycle management and security architectures for Agentic AI systems utilizing Non-Human Identities (NHI). Taxonomy of Research Areas: 1. Autonomous Agent Identity & Zero Trust Architecture: Developing robust security frameworks applied specifically to autonomous AI agents. Key technical mechanisms include: - Agent Attestation: Cryptographic verification of an AI agent's state and identity. - Access Control: Enforcement of granular agent permissions and authorization rules. - Automated Auditing: Continuous monitoring of agent behavior. - Security Paradigm: Strict adherence to Zero Trust principles for all Non-Human Identities. - Agent Identity/Authentication/Authorization: Providing identity to agent via tools such as verifiable credentials and Oauth techniques for authentication and authorization 2. Cryptographic Provenance & Data Authenticity: Validating the outputs and network actions of AI systems using cryptographic trust models. Key implementations include: - C2PA (Coalition for Content Provenance and Authenticity): Establishing verifiable digital provenance. - Verifiable Credentials (VC): Decentralized identity verification for agents. - Trusted Networks: Securing the communication layers between multi-agent systems. 3. Identity Infrastructure & Protocol Interoperability: Building the underlying infrastructure for agent identity lifecycle automation. Focus areas include: - Certificate Automation: Advancing automated Public Key Infrastructure (PKI) management using the ACME (Automated Certificate Management Environment) protocol. - Standards Compliance: Ensuring seamless interoperability with established identity providers (IdP) and authentication protocols, specifically IETF OAuth, OpenID Connect (OIDC), PingIdentity, and Microsoft Entra ID. 领域： 网络安全、人工智能、数字身份 核心研究目标： 利用非人类身份（NHI）的代理式人工智能（Agentic AI）系统的生命周期管理与安全架构。 研究领域分类： 1. 自主代理身份与零信任架构 (Autonomous Agent Identity & Zero Trust Architecture)： 专门针对自主人工智能代理开发稳健的安全框架。关键技术机制包括： • 代理证明 (Agent Attestation)： 对人工智能代理的状态和身份进行密码学验证。 • 访问控制 (Access Control)： 执行细粒度的代理权限和授权规则。 • 自动化审计 (Automated Auditing)： 对代理行为进行持续监控。 • 安全范式 (Security Paradigm)： 对所有非人类身份严格遵守零信任（Zero Trust）原则。 • 代理身份/认证/授权： 通过可验证凭证（Verifiable Credentials）和 OAuth 技术等工具为代理配置身份，以实现身份验证与授权。 2. 密码学来源与数据真实性 (Cryptographic Provenance & Data Authenticity)： 使用密码学信任模型验证人工智能系统的输出和网络行为。关键实现包括： • C2PA（内容来源和真实性联盟）： 建立可验证的数字来源。 • 可验证凭证 (Verifiable Credentials, VC)： 针对代理的去中心化身份验证。 • 可信网络 (Trusted Networks)： 保护多代理系统之间的通信层安全。 3. 身份基础设施与协议互操作性 (Identity Infrastructure & Protocol Interoperability)： 构建用于代理身份生命周期自动化的底层基础设施。重点领域包括： • 证书自动化 (Certificate Automation)： 使用 ACME（自动证书管理环境）协议推进自动化的公钥基础设施（PKI）管理。 • 标准合规 (Standards Compliance)： 确保与既定的身份提供商（IdP）和身份验证协议实现无缝互操作，特别是 IETF OAuth、OpenID Connect (OIDC)、PingIdentity 和 Microsoft Entra ID。

Security Operations Centre (SOC)

安全运营中心

Research focus

研究方向

Domain: Artificial Intelligence Security, Operating System (OS) Security, Mobile & Endpoint Security, Data Security & Privacy Protection Core Research Objective: Monitoring, analyzing, and deriving strategic insights from industry intelligence to benchmark HarmonyOS endpoint security against major ecosystems (Apple iOS, Google Android, Microsoft Windows). The research spans foundational OS security, data privacy frameworks, and emerging technologies at the intersection of AI (LLMs, Agents) and cybersecurity within edge-cloud environments. Taxonomy of Research Areas: 1. OS Security & Ecosystem Benchmarking: Tracking and analyzing security architectures, system-level implementations, and strategic shifts from industry-leading OS providers (Apple, Google, Microsoft) to establish comprehensive competitive intelligence and baseline standards for HarmonyOS. 2. Data Security & Privacy Protection: Investigating robust data protection mechanisms, local privacy boundaries, data leakage prevention (DLP), and compliance across mobile and desktop endpoint environments. 3. Industry Intelligence & AI Security Landscape: Tracking and analyzing global technology trends, security announcements, and strategic shifts from industry-leading organizations (such as Google, OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, Anthropic, etc.). 4. Advanced AI/Agent Security Technologies & Defenses: Evaluating how leading ecosystems secure their large language models and autonomous agent workflows against adversarial attacks, prompt injections, and system-level exploitation. 5. Threat Vectors, Vulnerabilities & Data Privacy: Investigating novel threat vectors, exploitation techniques, model vulnerabilities, and risk factors including privacy leakage and enterprise data breaches within AI deployments. 6. On-Device Agent & Endpoint Security: Exploring specialized security architectures for deploying autonomous AI agents natively on end-user devices (Mobile/IoT), ensuring strict access control, edge-cloud secure communication, and robust threat mitigation frameworks. 领域： 人工智能安全、操作系统 (OS) 安全、移动与端侧安全、数据安全与隐私保护 核心研究目标： 持续追踪、分析行业情报并获取战略洞察，将鸿蒙端侧安全与主流操作系统生态（苹果 iOS、谷歌 Android、微软 Windows）进行全面对标。研究范围涵盖底层操作系统安全、数据隐私框架以及人工智能（大语言模型、智能体）与网络安全交叉领域在端云协同环境下的前沿技术。 研究领域分类： 1. OS 安全与生态对标 (OS Security & Ecosystem Benchmarking)： 追踪和分析行业领先操作系统提供商（苹果、谷歌、微软）的安全架构、系统级实现与战略演进，为鸿蒙系统建立全面的竞争情报体系与基线标准。 2. 数据安全与隐私保护 (Data Security & Privacy Protection)： 研究移动与桌面终端环境下的高强度数据保护机制、本地隐私控制边界、防数据泄露 (DLP) 技术以及安全合规性。 3. 行业情报与 AI 安全态势： 追踪和分析全球头部科技企业（如 Google、OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, Anthropic 等）在安全领域的最新行业动态、技术发布与战略演进。 4. 先进 AI/Agent 安全技术与防御 (Advanced AI/Agent Security Technologies & Defenses)： 评估领先厂商如何保障其大语言模型与自主智能体工作流的安全，抵御对抗性攻击、提示词注入以及系统级漏洞利用。 5. 威胁向量、漏洞与数据隐私： 研究 AI 部署中出现的新型威胁向量、漏洞利用技术、模型自身缺陷，以及隐私泄露和企业数据泄露等高危风险。 6. 端侧 Agent 与终端安全 (On-Device Agent & Endpoint Security)： 探索在终端用户设备（移动端/物联网）上原生部署自主 AI 智能体所需的专属安全架构，确保严格的访问控制、端云安全通信以及稳健的威胁缓解框架。
