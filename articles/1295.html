Global Convergence of Wasserstein Policy Gradient for Entropy-Regularized Reinforcement Learning

Wasserstein策略梯度在熵正则化强化学习中的全局收敛性

rlhf AI & Machine Learning

Zhaoyu Zhu, Rui Gao, Shuang Li

Summary

This paper proves global convergence of Wasserstein policy gradient for entropy-regularized RL by exploiting Bellman recursion to replace convexity, establishing a distributional Polyak-Łojasiewicz condition.

摘要

本文证明了Wasserstein策略梯度在熵正则化强化学习中的全局收敛性，通过利用Bellman递归结构替代凸性假设，建立了分布层面的Polyak-Łojasiewicz条件。该工作为连续动作空间中基于最优传输的策略优化提供了首个非渐近收敛保证。

Deep Note

Deep Note — wpg-convergence

Key Takeaways - WPG for entropy-regularized RL converges globally despite non-convexity, using Bellman structure instead of convexity. - A Bellman resolvent identity connects value improvement to relative Fisher information. - Soft Bellman residual admits a statewise KL representation with respect to a Gibbs policy. - Uniform log-Sobolev inequality for the evolving Gibbs family yields a distributional Polyak-Łojasiewicz condition. - Discrete-time Langevin update achieves geometric contraction up to a discretization bias.

1) Why-read

2) CRGP

C — Context

Policy gradient methods in RL often rely on Euclidean or KL geometry, but Wasserstein geometry is natural for continuous action spaces. Existing global convergence analyses for PG or natural PG do not transfer to Wasserstein geometry, and prior OT-based results are asymptotic or lack entropy regularization.

R — Related work

  • Euclidean policy gradient (REINFORCE, Sutton et al.)
  • Natural policy gradient and trust-region methods (Kakade, TRPO, PPO)
  • Wasserstein policy optimization via JKO schemes (Zhang et al.)
  • Mean-field Langevin dynamics for convex objectives (Chizat, Mei et al.)
  • Soft Bellman operators and maximum-entropy RL (Haarnoja et al., Geist et al.)

G — Gap

Global convergence of Wasserstein policy gradient with Langevin dynamics for entropy-regularized RL was open. Standard Langevin analyses assume convex objectives, but the RL objective depends on policy through Bellman recursion, destroying convexity. Existing OT-based results are asymptotic or lack entropy regularization.

P — Proposal

The authors develop a global convergence theory by replacing convexity with Bellman-based arguments: soft Bellman residual as KL divergence, Bellman contraction relating residual to optimality gap, and a Bellman resolvent identity connecting value improvement to Fisher information. Combined with a uniform log-Sobolev inequality, this yields a distributional Polyak-Łojasiewicz condition and geometric contraction up to discretization bias.

4) Experiments

Main results

The paper is theoretical; no experimental results are reported.

Ablation

The analysis isolates three Bellman mechanisms (residual-KL, contraction, resolvent identity) and shows they collectively replace convexity. Uniform bounds on value functions, Q-functions, drift Lipschitz constants, and moments are established to control discretization error.

Limitations

['Analysis is limited to entropy-regularized RL with continuous actions and assumes full-support initial state distribution.', 'Discrete-time convergence includes a bias term from discretization that does not vanish with step size.', 'Uniform log-Sobolev inequality is assumed for the Gibbs family, which may not hold for all MDPs.']

5) Why it matters

  1. Provides first non-asymptotic global convergence guarantee for Wasserstein policy gradient with Langevin dynamics.
  2. Demonstrates that Bellman recursion induces a PL-type geometry even when the objective is not convex.
  3. Offers a template for analyzing other non-convex RL algorithms via Bellman structure rather than convexity.

1) 阅读理由

本文通过Bellman递归替代凸性，建立了分布Polyak-Łojasiewicz条件，证明了Wasserstein策略梯度在熵正则化RL中的全局收敛性。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

背景：强化学习中的策略梯度方法通常依赖欧几里得或KL几何，但Wasserstein几何更适用于连续动作空间。现有PG或自然PG的全局收敛分析无法迁移到Wasserstein几何，而基于最优传输的先前结果要么是渐近的，要么缺乏熵正则化。

相关工作：欧几里得策略梯度（REINFORCE, Sutton等人）、自然策略梯度与信赖域方法（Kakade, TRPO, PPO）、基于JKO方案的Wasserstein策略优化（Zhang等人）、凸目标的平均场Langevin动力学（Chizat, Mei等人）、软Bellman算子与最大熵RL（Haarnoja等人, Geist等人）。

差距：Wasserstein策略梯度结合Langevin动力学在熵正则化RL中的全局收敛性尚未解决。标准Langevin分析假设凸目标，但RL目标通过Bellman递归依赖于策略，破坏了凸性。现有最优传输结果要么是渐近的，要么缺乏熵正则化。

提议：作者通过用基于Bellman的论证替代凸性，发展了全局收敛理论：软Bellman残差作为KL散度、Bellman收缩将残差与最优性差距关联、Bellman预解恒等式将值函数改进与Fisher信息联系起来。结合均匀对数Sobolev不等式，导出了分布Polyak-Łojasiewicz条件和离散化偏差下的几何收缩。

3) 实验

本文为理论性论文，未报告实验数据。

消融实验

分析隔离了三种Bellman机制（残差-KL、收缩、预解恒等式），并表明它们共同替代了凸性。建立了值函数、Q函数、漂移Lipschitz常数和矩的均匀界以控制离散化误差。

局限性

['分析仅限于连续动作空间的熵正则化RL，并假设初始状态分布具有全支撑。', '离散时间收敛包含一个不随步长消失的离散化偏差项。', '假设Gibbs族满足均匀对数Sobolev不等式，该假设可能不适用于所有MDP。']

4) 重要性

  1. 首次为Wasserstein策略梯度结合Langevin动力学提供了非渐近全局收敛保证。
  2. 展示了即使目标非凸，Bellman递归也能诱导出PL型几何结构。
  3. 为通过Bellman结构而非凸性分析其他非凸RL算法提供了模板。
