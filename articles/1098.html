Generative AI Advertising as a Problem of Trustworthy Commercial Intervention

生成式AI广告：可信商业干预问题

Jingyi Qiu, Qiaozhu Mei · 2026-05-19 · arxiv

Summary

Generative AI advertising shifts from content placement to process intervention, creating a trustworthiness crisis that current detection and disclosure frameworks cannot address.

摘要

本文重新定义了生成式AI广告问题，指出其从内容投放转向过程干预，带来了现有检测与披露框架无法应对的信任危机。论文提出了一个四层影响分类法（产品提及、信息框架、行为重定向、长期偏好塑造），并分析了各层在不同架构中的实现方式及开放问题。

1) Why-read

Generative AI advertising shifts from content placement to process intervention, creating a trustworthiness crisis that current detection and disclosure frameworks cannot address.

2) CRGP

C — Context

Major AI platforms (Google, OpenAI, Microsoft) have deployed first-generation advertising with visible boundaries between ads and organic responses. However, mechanism design literature proposes deeper integrations like token-level auctions and RAG-based segment auctions, while empirical studies show users often fail to detect ads woven into LLM outputs and that such ads can match or outperform ad-free outputs on perceived helpfulness.

R — Related work

  • Duetting et al. (2024) - token-level auctions for LLM advertising
  • Hajiaghayi et al. (2024) - RAG-based segment auctions
  • Dubey et al. (2024) - sponsored LLM summaries
  • Soumalias et al. (2024) - aggregation of advertiser preferences over generated replies
  • Tang et al. (2025) - ads in chatbot outputs often go undetected
  • Salvi et al. (2026) - adversarial amplification through prompt manipulation
  • Aggarwal et al. (2024) - generative engine optimization increases source visibility
  • Tohidi et al. (2025) - LLM-generated framings shift attitudes even with constant facts
  • Allouah et al. (2026) - shopping agents exhibit position biases and platform endorsements

G — Gap

Existing work focuses on content placement and visible ad slots, but generative AI enables commercial influence through less observable channels like framing, behavioral redirection, and preference shaping. There is no framework for detecting, measuring, or disclosing these deeper forms of influence, and the trustworthiness requirements (attribution, measurability, contestability) become harder as influence moves from product mentions to latent variable interventions.

P — Proposal

The paper reframes generative AI advertising as a problem of trustworthy intervention in the generative process, introducing a four-tier taxonomy: product mentions (Tier 1), information framing (Tier 2), behavioral redirection (Tier 3), and long-term preference shaping (Tier 4). It analyzes how these tiers instantiate across modalities and architectures (RAG, agentic pipelines) and identifies open problems for detection, influence estimation, and contestability at each tier.

5) Why it matters

  1. Directly addresses the tension between monetization and trust in AI assistants, which is critical for user welfare and platform sustainability.
  2. Provides a structured vocabulary (four tiers of influence) that can guide future research on detection, measurement, and disclosure of commercial influence in generative systems.
  3. Connects to broader AI safety concerns about hidden manipulation and user autonomy, especially in high-stakes decision contexts.
  4. Highlights cascade effects in agentic pipelines where upstream commercial interventions constrain downstream outcomes, relevant for multi-agent and tool-use systems.

6) Next steps

  • [ ] Design disclosure mechanisms that go beyond visible ad labels to inform users about framing and behavioral influence.
  • [ ] Empirically measure the prevalence and behavioral impact of different influence tiers in deployed systems like ChatGPT and Copilot.
  • [ ] Explore regulatory frameworks that require platforms to characterize and disclose commercial influence in generative processes.

深度笔记

生成式AI广告：可信商业干预问题

主要结论 - 生成式AI广告的核心威胁在于通过不可观察的渠道（如框架效应、行为重定向）进行商业干预，而非传统广告位。 - 现有检测和披露框架仅适用于可见广告位，无法应对深层影响（Tier 2-4）。 - 论文提出四层分类法（产品提及、信息框架、行为重定向、长期偏好塑造），为后续研究提供结构化词汇。 - 实证表明用户常无法检测LLM输出中的广告，且此类广告在感知有用性上可匹敌或超越无广告输出。 - 在智能体管道中，上游商业干预会级联影响下游结果，对多智能体系统安全至关重要。

1) 阅读理由

核心主张：生成式AI广告从内容投放转向过程干预，造成现有框架无法解决的信任危机；关键观察：用户常无法检测LLM输出中的广告，且此类广告在感知有用性上可匹敌无广告输出。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

背景：主要AI平台（Google、OpenAI、Microsoft）已部署第一代广告，在广告与有机回复间有可见边界。但机制设计文献提出更深层整合（如token级拍卖、RAG分段拍卖），实证表明用户常无法检测LLM输出中的广告，且此类广告在感知有用性上可匹敌或超越无广告输出。

相关工作：Duetting等（2024）提出token级LLM广告拍卖；Hajiaghayi等（2024）提出RAG分段拍卖；Dubey等（2024）研究赞助LLM摘要；Soumalias等（2024）聚合广告主对生成回复的偏好；Tang等（2025）发现聊天机器人输出中的广告常未被检测；Salvi等（2026）研究通过提示操纵的对抗放大；Aggarwal等（2024）发现生成引擎优化增加来源可见性；Tohidi等（2025）发现LLM生成框架即使事实不变也能改变态度；Allouah等（2026）发现购物智能体存在位置偏差和平台背书。

差距：现有工作聚焦内容投放和可见广告位，但生成式AI通过框架效应、行为重定向、偏好塑造等不可观察渠道实现商业影响。缺乏检测、测量或披露这些深层影响的框架，且随着影响从产品提及转向潜在变量干预，可信要求（归因、可测量、可争议）变得更难。

提案：论文将生成式AI广告重新定义为生成过程中的可信干预问题，提出四层分类法：产品提及（Tier 1）、信息框架（Tier 2）、行为重定向（Tier 3）、长期偏好塑造（Tier 4）。分析各层在不同模态和架构（RAG、智能体管道）中的实现方式，并识别各层在检测、影响估计和可争议性方面的开放问题。

3) 实验

本文为概念框架和分类法论文，无实验。引用的实证结果包括：对973个电商网站12个月的分析发现，有机ChatGPT引荐的转化率和每次会话收入高于付费社交；嵌入聊天机器人输出的广告常未被检测，且在感知有用性上可匹敌或超越无广告输出。
