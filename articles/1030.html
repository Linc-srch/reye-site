Lyria 3 Pro: Create longer tracks in more

Lyria 3 Pro: Create longer tracks in more

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind announces Lyria 3 Pro, an advanced music generation model that creates tracks up to three minutes long with structural awareness of intros, verses, choruses, and bridges. The model is integrated into Vertex AI, Google AI Studio, Gemini API, Google Vids, the Gemini app, and ProducerAI, enabling scalable music production for developers, businesses, and creators. Google emphasizes responsible development through partnerships with musicians, use of licensed training data, non-mimicry safeguards, and SynthID watermarking. Lyria 3 Pro aims to make high-quality, customizable music generation accessible across multiple Google products.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布 Lyria 3 Pro，这是一款先进的音乐生成模型，可生成长达三分钟、具备结构感知能力的曲目，支持前奏、主歌、副歌和桥段。该模型已集成至 Vertex AI、Google AI Studio、Gemini API、Google Vids、Gemini 应用及 ProducerAI，为开发者、企业和创作者提供可扩展的音乐制作能力。Google 强调与音乐人合作、使用授权训练数据、非模仿保护机制及 SynthID 水印，以负责任的方式推动 AI 音乐工具的发展。
