How CISOs Should Prep for Agentic-Ready AI BOMs

How CISOs Should Prep for Agentic-Ready AI BOMs

content-management supply-chain-attack zero-trust
启示 · Insight

For Digital Identity & Trust research, AI BOMs are a critical mechanism for establishing cryptographic provenance and automated auditing of agentic AI systems. They provide a structured inventory that can be linked to Non-Human Identities (NHIs), enabling granular access control and Zero Trust enforcement. For System Security, AI BOMs extend software supply-chain security (SBOM) concepts to AI components, introducing new attack surfaces in dependency management and build-system integrity that require memory-safe tooling and isolation.

对于数字身份与信任研究，AI BOM是建立代理式AI系统的密码学来源和自动化审计的关键机制。它提供了一个结构化清单，可与非人类身份（NHI）关联，从而实现细粒度访问控制和零信任执行。对于系统安全，AI BOM将软件供应链安全（SBOM）概念扩展到AI组件，在依赖关系管理和构建系统完整性方面引入了新的攻击面，需要内存安全工具和隔离机制。

Summary

The article advises CISOs to prepare for agentic AI by adopting AI Bill of Materials (AI BOMs) to inventory AI components, track dependencies, and manage supply chain risks as AI agents become more autonomous.

摘要

文章建议首席信息安全官（CISO）通过采用AI物料清单（AI BOM）来为代理式AI做准备，以盘点AI组件、跟踪依赖关系并管理供应链风险，因为AI代理正变得越来越自主。
