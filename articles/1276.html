How CISOs Should Prep for Agentic-Ready AI BOMs

How CISOs Should Prep for Agentic-Ready AI BOMs

content-management supply-chain-attack zero-trust AI Safety & Alignment Supply Chain Security
Summary

This article advises Chief Information Security Officers (CISOs) on preparing for agentic AI by adopting AI Bill of Materials (AI BOMs). It emphasizes the need to inventory AI components, track dependencies, and manage supply chain risks as AI agents become more autonomous. The piece outlines practical steps for integrating AI BOMs into existing security frameworks to ensure transparency and accountability in AI-driven operations.

摘要

本文建议首席信息安全官（CISO）通过采用AI Bill of Materials（AI BOM）来为自主AI代理做好准备。文章强调需要清点AI组件、跟踪依赖关系并管理供应链风险，同时概述了将AI BOM集成到现有安全框架中的实用步骤，以确保AI驱动操作的透明度和问责制。
