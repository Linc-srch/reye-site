Strengthening Singapore’s AI Future: A New National Partnership

Strengthening Singapore’s AI Future: A New National Partnership

2026-05-20 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind announced a national AI partnership with Singapore to advance healthcare, education, scientific discovery, and sustainability. The collaboration includes AI co-clinician research, pandemic preparedness using AlphaFold, a Gemma-powered running assistant for blind athletes, and training on agentic AI tools. Google also provides Gemini for Education to all educators and launches the 'AI for the Planet' accelerator. The partnership emphasizes responsible AI through safety benchmarks with IMDA and MLCommons, aiming to generate S$3.3 billion in economic value by 2040.

摘要

Google DeepMind与新加坡建立国家AI合作伙伴关系，聚焦医疗、教育、科学发现和可持续发展。合作包括AI co-clinician研究、利用AlphaFold进行大流行病准备、为视障运动员开发Gemma驱动的跑步助手，以及agentic AI工具培训。Google向所有教育工作者提供Gemini for Education，并启动'AI for the Planet'加速器。该合作强调通过IMDA和MLCommons制定多模态安全基准，预计到2040年创造33亿新元经济价值。
