Accelerating discovery in India through AI-powered science and education

Accelerating discovery in India through AI-powered science and education

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind announces a National Partnership for AI with Indian government bodies and institutions to accelerate AI-powered science and education. The collaboration provides access to frontier AI models like AlphaGenome, AI Co-scientist, and Earth AI for researchers, alongside a $30 million Google.org Impact Challenge for AI for Science. In education, partnerships with Atal Tinkering Labs and PM Publishers integrate Gemini into classrooms, transforming textbooks into interactive experiences and supporting teachers. The initiative also extends to agriculture and energy, using AI for crop productivity and renewable energy forecasting. This partnership aims to broaden AI access, serving national priorities and fostering global AI collaboration.

摘要

Google DeepMind宣布与印度政府机构建立国家AI合作伙伴关系，以加速AI驱动的科学和教育。合作提供AlphaGenome、AI Co-scientist和Earth AI等前沿模型，并设立3000万美元的Google.org Impact Challenge支持AI for Science。教育方面，与Atal Tinkering Labs和PM Publishers合作，将Gemini融入课堂，将静态教科书转化为互动学习体验。此外，AI还被用于农业和能源领域，提升作物产量和可再生能源预测。该伙伴关系旨在扩大AI访问，服务国家优先事项，并促进全球AI合作。
