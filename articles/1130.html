Gemini 3 Deep Think: Advancing science, research and engineering

Gemini 3 Deep Think: Advancing science, research and engineering

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google has released a major upgrade to Gemini 3 Deep Think, a specialized reasoning mode designed to advance science, research, and engineering. The model achieves state-of-the-art results on rigorous benchmarks, including 48.4% on Humanity's Last Exam and 84.6% on ARC-AGI-2, and demonstrates gold-medal performance on the International Math Olympiad 2025 as well as physics and chemistry olympiads. Early testers have used it to identify subtle logical flaws in peer-reviewed mathematics papers and optimize crystal growth fabrication methods. The updated Deep Think is now available to Google AI Ultra subscribers in the Gemini app and via the Gemini API through an early access program.

摘要

Google 发布了 Gemini 3 Deep Think 的重大升级，这是一种专门用于推进科学、研究和工程的推理模式。该模型在多个严格基准测试中取得了领先成果，包括 Humanity's Last Exam 上达到 48.4%，ARC-AGI-2 上达到 84.6%，并在国际数学奥林匹克 2025 以及物理和化学奥林匹克中展现了金牌级表现。早期测试者已利用它识别同行评审数学论文中的细微逻辑缺陷，并优化晶体生长制造方法。更新后的 Deep Think 现面向 Google AI Ultra 订阅者开放，并通过 Gemini API 提供早期访问计划。
