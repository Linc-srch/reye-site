Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS: the next generation of expressive AI speech

Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS: the next generation of expressive AI speech

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google has launched Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS, a new text-to-speech model offering improved controllability, expressivity, and speech quality. It introduces audio tags that allow users to adjust vocal style, pace, and delivery via natural language commands embedded in text. The model supports over 70 languages, features native multi-speaker dialogue, and achieves a high Elo score on the Artificial Analysis TTS leaderboard. All generated audio is watermarked with SynthID to prevent misinformation. Developers can access it via Google AI Studio, Vertex AI, and Google Vids, with configurable controls for fine-tuning and consistent voice export.

摘要

Google 发布了 Gemini 3.1 Flash TTS，这是一款新的文本转语音模型，提供了更强的可控性、表现力和语音质量。它引入了 audio tags，允许用户通过嵌入文本的自然语言指令调整发声风格、语速和表达方式。该模型支持超过 70 种语言，具备原生多说话人对话能力，并在 Artificial Analysis TTS 排行榜上取得了高 Elo 分数。所有生成的音频都使用 SynthID 水印以防止 misinformation。开发者可通过 Google AI Studio、Vertex AI 和 Google Vids 访问，并利用可配置的控制进行微调和一致的语音导出。
