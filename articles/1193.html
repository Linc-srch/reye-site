The next phase of OpenAI’s Education for Countries

The next phase of OpenAI’s Education for Countries

2026-05-21 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI announces the next phase of its Education for Countries initiative, expanding access to AI learning resources and tools for educators and students globally. The program aims to equip nations with the skills and knowledge needed to leverage AI technologies responsibly, focusing on curriculum development, teacher training, and ethical AI use. This phase includes partnerships with governments and educational institutions to create localized content and foster AI literacy, ensuring that countries can harness AI for economic growth and societal benefit while addressing safety and equity concerns.

摘要

OpenAI 宣布其 Education for Countries 计划进入下一阶段，旨在扩大全球教育者和学生获取 AI 学习资源与工具的渠道。该计划专注于课程开发、教师培训及伦理 AI 使用，通过与政府和教育机构合作创建本地化内容，提升 AI 素养，确保各国在应对安全与公平问题的同时，利用 AI 促进经济增长与社会福祉。
