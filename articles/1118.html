Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6: Powering real-world robotics tasks through enhanced embodied reasoning

Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6: Powering real-world robotics tasks through enhanced embodied reasoning

sparse-attention differentiable-routing hierarchical-attention AI & Machine Learning AI Safety & Alignment
Summary

Google DeepMind introduces Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6, a significant upgrade to its embodied reasoning model for robotics. The model enhances spatial reasoning, multi-view understanding, and success detection, enabling robots to perform complex tasks like instrument reading, pointing, and counting with unprecedented precision. It integrates with tools like Google Search and vision-language-action models, and supports agentic vision for code execution. Benchmark results show improvements over previous versions. The model is available via the Gemini API and Google AI Studio, with a focus on safety and real-world applications such as facility inspection in collaboration with Boston Dynamics.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布了 Gemini Robotics-ER 1.6，这是其具身推理模型的重要升级。该模型增强了空间推理、多视角理解和成功检测能力，使机器人能够以空前精度执行仪表读取、指向和计数等复杂任务。它集成了 Google Search 和 vision-language-action models 等工具，并支持 agentic vision 进行代码执行。基准测试结果显示其性能优于前代版本。该模型通过 Gemini API 和 Google AI Studio 提供，注重安全性，并与 Boston Dynamics 合作应用于设施检查等真实场景。
