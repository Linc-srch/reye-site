Introducing OpenAI for Singapore

Summary

OpenAI announces its expansion into Singapore, establishing a new office to support the growing demand for AI technologies in the Asia-Pacific region. The move aims to foster local partnerships, advance AI research, and provide better access to OpenAI's tools and models for businesses and developers in Singapore. This strategic expansion reflects OpenAI's commitment to global collaboration and responsible AI deployment.

摘要

OpenAI宣布在新加坡设立新办公室，以支持亚太地区对AI技术日益增长的需求。此举旨在促进本地合作、推进AI研究，并为新加坡的企业和开发者提供更好的OpenAI工具和模型访问。这一战略扩张体现了OpenAI对全球合作和负责任AI部署的承诺。
