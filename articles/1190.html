Fast-tracking genetic leads to reverse cellular aging

Fast-tracking genetic leads to reverse cellular aging

2026-05-20  ·  deepmind_blog  ·  Original →

Summary

Google DeepMind's Co-Scientist AI accelerates aging research by analyzing vast genetic screening data and scientific literature. Biologists Omar Abudayyeh and Jonathan Gootenberg use it to identify genetic factors that reverse cellular senescence, proposing over 20 novel leads, with lab tests validating several that drove cells to a younger state. Co-Scientist also reduces data analysis time from six months to days, enabling faster hypothesis testing and discovery.

摘要

Google DeepMind的Co-Scientist AI通过分析大量基因筛选数据和科学文献，加速衰老研究。生物学家Omar Abudayyeh和Jonathan Gootenberg利用它识别逆转细胞衰老的遗传因子，提出了20多个新线索，实验室验证了其中几个成功使细胞恢复年轻状态。Co-Scientist还将数据分析时间从六个月缩短至几天，加快了假设测试和发现进程。
