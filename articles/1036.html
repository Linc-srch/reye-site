A new way to express yourself: Gemini can now create music

A new way to express yourself: Gemini can now create music

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google's Gemini app now integrates Lyria 3, Google DeepMind's latest music generation model, allowing users to create 30-second tracks from text prompts or uploaded images. The feature offers improved creative control, automatic lyrics generation, and realistic audio. All outputs are watermarked with SynthID for AI identification. Available in beta for users 18+ in multiple languages, with higher limits for subscribers. The tool aims to foster creative expression rather than produce masterpieces, and includes safeguards against artist mimicry and copyright infringement.

摘要

Google的Gemini应用集成了DeepMind最新的Lyria 3音乐生成模型，用户可通过文本或图片生成30秒音乐片段。该功能支持自动歌词生成、风格与节奏控制，并输出更逼真的音频。所有生成内容均嵌入SynthID水印以标识AI来源。Beta版面向18岁以上用户开放，支持多语言，订阅用户享有更高使用额度。工具旨在促进创意表达，并包含防止模仿艺术家及侵权的保护措施。
