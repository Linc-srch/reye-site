Summary

OpenAI is launching the Campus Network initiative to partner with student clubs worldwide, offering hands-on AI learning, support for events and research, early tool access, and global leadership connections. An interest form collects club details, focus areas, AI usage, and desired opportunities like workshops, ambassador programs, and credits.
