May 7, 2026AlignmentDonating our open-source alignment tool

Summary

Anthropic has announced the donation of its open-source alignment evaluation tool, Petri, to Meridian Labs, an AI evaluation nonprofit. Originally launched in October 2025, Petri is a toolbox of alignment tests for large language models, detecting tendencies like deception and sycophancy. The updated Petri 3.0 introduces major architectural changes for adaptability, a realism-enhancing add-on called Dish, and deeper integration with Anthropic's Bloom tool. By transferring ownership to Meridian Labs, Anthropic ensures Petri remains independent and neutral, fostering trust across industry and governments. This move parallels Anthropic's earlier donation of the Model Context Protocol to the Linux Foundation, reinforcing the importance of open, credible AI safety evaluation.

摘要

Anthropic宣布将其开源对齐评估工具Petri捐赠给AI评估非营利组织Meridian Labs。Petri是一个用于大型语言模型的对齐测试工具箱，可检测欺骗、迎合等倾向。新版本Petri 3.0增强了适应性、真实性（通过Dish插件）和深度（与Bloom集成）。捐赠旨在确保工具的独立性和可信度，推动AI安全评估的开放与中立。
