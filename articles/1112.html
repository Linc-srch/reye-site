Summary

AlphaEvolve, a Gemini-powered coding agent by Google DeepMind, has scaled its impact across diverse fields over the past year. It improves DNA sequencing accuracy via DeepConsensus, boosts electricity grid optimization by increasing feasible solutions from 14% to over 88%, and enhances natural disaster prediction by 5%. In research, it achieves 10x lower error in quantum circuits on Willow, solves Erdős problems, and breaks records in Traveling Salesman and Ramsey Numbers. It optimizes TPU design, cache policies, and Google Spanner, reducing write amplification by 20%. Commercial applications include doubling Klarna's training speed, improving Substrate's lithography runtime, and saving FM Logistic 15,000 km annually.

摘要

AlphaEvolve是Google DeepMind基于Gemini的编码智能体，过去一年在多个领域扩大了影响。它通过DeepConsensus将DNA测序错误减少30%，将电网优化中可行解比例从14%提升至88%以上，并将自然灾害预测准确率提高5%。在量子物理中，它使Willow处理器上的量子电路误差降低10倍；解决了Erdős问题，并改进了Traveling Salesman Problem和Ramsey Numbers的下界。它还优化了TPU设计、缓存策略和Google Spanner，将写入放大降低20%。商业应用中，Klarna训练速度翻倍，Substrate光刻运行时速提升多倍，FM Logistic年节省超15,000公里路程。
