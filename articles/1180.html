Co-Scientist: A multi-agent AI partner to accelerate research

Summary

Google DeepMind introduces Co-Scientist, a multi-agent AI system built with Gemini that accelerates scientific discovery by generating, debating, and evolving hypotheses. The system uses specialized agents organized into three phases: generating ideas, debating them through simulated peer review and tournaments, and evolving top candidates. Validated in life sciences, it has helped uncover drug repurposing candidates for liver fibrosis, propose RNA-based approaches for ALS, and identify genetic leads for reversing cellular aging. Co-Scientist integrates web search, specialized databases, and tools like AlphaFold, and is available as an experimental tool for researchers.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布了 Co-Scientist，一个基于 Gemini 的多智能体 AI 系统，通过生成、辩论和演化假设来加速科学发现。系统包含生成、反思、排名、进化等专门智能体，并采用“想法锦标赛”机制验证和优化假设。在生命科学领域，该系统已帮助发现肝纤维化药物再利用候选方案、提出 ALS 的 RNA 疗法思路，并识别逆转细胞衰老的基因线索。Co-Scientist 整合了 web 搜索、ChEMBL、UniProt 和 AlphaFold 等工具，现已作为实验工具向研究人员开放。
