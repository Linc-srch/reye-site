OpenAI, Grupo Folha and Grupo UOL announce strategic content partnership

OpenAI, Grupo Folha and Grupo UOL announce strategic content partnership

Summary

OpenAI has announced a strategic content partnership with Brazilian media groups Grupo Folha and Grupo UOL. The collaboration aims to integrate high-quality journalism from these publishers into OpenAI's products, including ChatGPT, while ensuring proper attribution and support for the news industry. This deal reflects a growing trend of AI companies licensing content from publishers to improve model training and output accuracy, addressing copyright and fair use concerns.

摘要

OpenAI宣布与巴西媒体集团Grupo Folha和Grupo UOL建立战略内容合作伙伴关系，旨在将其高质量新闻整合到ChatGPT等产品中，确保内容归属并支持新闻行业。此举反映了AI公司通过许可出版商内容来改进模型训练和输出准确性的趋势，同时应对版权和合理使用问题。
