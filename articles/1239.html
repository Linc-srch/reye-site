Summary

Virgin Atlantic leveraged OpenAI's Codex to enhance test coverage, accelerate legacy code refactoring, and ship a revamped mobile app with zero critical defects during the high-risk Christmas travel period. Codex reduced legacy codebase sizes by 78-80% and cut refactoring time from two weeks to 30 minutes. The tool also enabled non-engineering teams to prototype internal applications directly against the data warehouse. The case demonstrates how AI-assisted coding can improve software quality and delivery velocity in enterprise settings, though challenges remain in scaling adoption across the full software development lifecycle.
