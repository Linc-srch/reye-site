Helping ChatGPT better recognize context in sensitive conversations

Helping ChatGPT better recognize context in sensitive conversations

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI introduces safety updates to help ChatGPT better recognize context in sensitive conversations, particularly when risk emerges over time. The updates improve the model's ability to identify subtle or evolving cues of distress, such as suicide, self-harm, or harm-to-others, and respond appropriately by de-escalating, refusing harmful details, or redirecting to safer alternatives. Key innovations include safety summaries—short factual notes about prior safety-relevant context across conversations—and enhanced training with mental health experts. Internal evaluations show significant improvements in safe-response performance, with up to 52% improvement in harm-to-others cases on GPT-5.5 Instant, without degrading ordinary conversation quality.

摘要

OpenAI 推出安全更新，帮助 ChatGPT 在敏感对话中更好地识别上下文，特别是当风险随时间逐渐显现时。这些更新提升了模型识别自杀、自残或伤害他人等微妙或演变中 distress 信号的能力，并通过缓和局势、拒绝有害细节或引导至更安全选项来做出适当回应。关键创新包括 safety summaries——关于跨对话先前安全相关上下文的简短事实记录——以及结合心理健康专家的强化训练。内部评估显示，在 GPT-5.5 Instant 上，伤害他人场景的安全响应性能提升了 52%，且不影响普通对话质量。
