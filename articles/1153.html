KoRe: Compact Knowledge Representations for Large Language Models

KoRe：面向大型语言模型的紧凑知识表示

Davide Cavicchini, Fausto Giunchiglia, Jacopo Staiano

Summary

KoRe proposes a token-efficient method to inject structured knowledge from knowledge graphs into LLMs via discrete tokens, achieving competitive QA performance with up to 10x fewer tokens.

摘要

本文提出KoRe方法，通过方向残差向量量化将知识图谱的1跳子图压缩为离散令牌，实现高效注入大语言模型。相比文本序列化，令牌使用量减少高达10倍，且在三个问答基准上取得竞争性能，并支持对未见实体的泛化。

Deep Note

Deep Note — kore

Key Takeaways - KoRe encodes 1-hop knowledge graph sub-graphs into compact discrete tokens for LLM injection. - Achieves up to 10x reduction in token usage compared to textual serialization of KGs. - Uses directional residual vector quantization (RVQ) to avoid codebook collapse and compress graph embeddings. - Competitive performance on three QA benchmarks with a frozen Qwen3-8B backbone and LoRA. - Enables generalization to unseen entities without retraining knowledge graph embeddings.

1) Why-read

KoRe proposes a token-efficient method to inject structured knowledge from knowledge graphs into LLMs via discrete tokens, achieving competitive QA performance with up to 10x fewer tokens.

2) CRGP

C — Context

LLMs encode world knowledge in their parameters, making them opaque, hard to update, and prone to hallucinations. Knowledge graphs (KGs) offer structured, interpretable, and updatable facts, but integrating them into LLMs typically requires extensive retraining or token-inefficient textual serialization.

R — Related work

  • RAG and KG-RAG mitigate hallucinations but inflate context windows and inference costs.
  • K-Adapter and KnowLA use lightweight adapters but require pre-computed embeddings, limiting generalization.
  • GraphToken, TEA-GLM, and GQT inject graph structure as soft tokens but target graph tasks, not LLM factual grounding.
  • ConceptFormer injects concept vectors into small models (GPT-2 0.1B) but not validated on modern LLMs.

G — Gap

Existing KG integration methods either require extensive retraining, are limited to small models, or suffer from token inefficiency. No prior work has shown that discrete, compressed KG tokens can effectively ground large modern LLMs (e.g., 8B parameters) for question answering while generalizing to unseen entities.

P — Proposal

KoRe extracts 1-hop star sub-graphs from Wikidata, encodes them via a TransformerConv GNN with frozen sentence embeddings, compresses the graph summary into discrete tokens using directional residual vector quantization, and injects these tokens into a frozen Qwen3-8B backbone with LoRA. The key insight is that directional RVQ prevents codebook collapse and enables compact, discrete knowledge representation.

4) Experiments

Main results

KoRe is evaluated on three benchmarks: Tri-REx (accuracy 78.4%), WebQSP (F1 72.1%), and Mintaka (F1 65.3%), achieving competitive performance while using up to 10x fewer tokens than textual serialization baselines.

Ablation

Directional RVQ (cosine-similarity selection with component removal) prevents codebook collapse; standard RVQ led to collapse in preliminary experiments. Using Q=4 tokens yields the best trade-off between compression and accuracy.

Limitations

['Relies on 1-hop sub-graphs; multi-hop reasoning is not directly supported.', 'Graph extraction depends on Wikidata and entity linking quality.', 'Evaluation limited to question answering; other knowledge-intensive tasks not tested.']

5) Why it matters

  1. Token-efficient knowledge injection is critical for reducing inference cost in RAG-like systems.
  2. Discrete tokens enable interpretable and updatable knowledge grounding without retraining the LLM.
  3. The approach can be adapted to your work on grounding LLMs with structured knowledge for safety-critical applications.

主要结论 - KoRe将知识图谱的1跳子图编码为紧凑离散令牌，用于注入大语言模型。 - 相比文本序列化，令牌使用量减少高达10倍。 - 采用方向残差向量量化（RVQ）避免码本崩溃并压缩图嵌入。 - 在冻结Qwen3-8B骨干网络和LoRA微调下，三个问答基准上表现竞争。 - 无需重新训练知识图谱嵌入即可泛化到未见实体。

1) 阅读理由

KoRe提出一种令牌高效的方法，通过离散令牌将知识图谱的结构化知识注入大语言模型，在问答任务上以高达10倍的令牌减少实现竞争性能。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

上下文：大语言模型将世界知识编码在参数中，导致不透明、难以更新且易产生幻觉。知识图谱提供结构化、可解释且可更新的事实，但集成到大语言模型通常需要大量重新训练或令牌低效的文本序列化。 相关工作：RAG和KG-RAG缓解幻觉但膨胀上下文窗口和推理成本；K-Adapter和KnowLA使用轻量适配器但需预计算嵌入，限制泛化；GraphToken、TEA-GLM和GQT将图结构注入为软令牌但针对图任务而非大语言模型事实 grounding；ConceptFormer将概念向量注入小模型但未在现代大语言模型上验证。 差距：现有知识图谱集成方法要么需要大量重新训练，要么局限于小模型，要么令牌效率低。尚无工作表明离散、压缩的知识图谱令牌能有效 grounding 现代大语言模型（如8B参数）进行问答并泛化到未见实体。 提案：KoRe从Wikidata提取1跳星形子图，通过TransformerConv图神经网络和冻结句子嵌入编码，利用方向残差向量量化将图摘要压缩为离散令牌，并通过LoRA注入冻结的Qwen3-8B骨干网络。关键洞察是方向RVQ防止码本崩溃并实现紧凑、离散的知识表示。

3) 实验

KoRe在三个基准上评估：Tri-REx（准确率78.4%）、WebQSP（F1 72.1%）和Mintaka（F1 65.3%），实现竞争性能，同时令牌使用量比文本序列化基线减少高达10倍。

消融实验

方向RVQ（余弦相似度选择加分量移除）防止码本崩溃；标准RVQ在初步实验中导致崩溃。使用Q=4个令牌在压缩和准确率之间取得最佳权衡。

局限性

['依赖1跳子图，不支持多跳推理。', '图提取依赖Wikidata和实体链接质量。', '评估仅限于问答，未测试其他知识密集型任务。']

4) 重要性

  1. 令牌高效的知识注入对于降低RAG类系统的推理成本至关重要。
  2. 离散令牌无需重新训练大语言模型即可实现可解释且可更新的知识 grounding。
  3. 该方法可适用于在安全关键应用中用结构化知识 grounding 大语言模型的工作。
