OpenAI launches DeployCo to help businesses build around intelligence

OpenAI launches DeployCo to help businesses build around intelligence

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI announced the launch of DeployCo, a new company focused on helping organizations build and deploy AI systems for critical workflows. DeployCo will embed Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) into client organizations to redesign infrastructure and workflows around AI. OpenAI also agreed to acquire Tomoro, an applied AI consulting firm, bringing approximately 150 experienced engineers. The venture is backed by over $4 billion in initial investment from partners including TPG, Advent, and Bain Capital. DeployCo is majority-owned by OpenAI and aims to accelerate enterprise AI adoption and ensure AGI benefits humanity.

摘要

OpenAI 宣布成立 DeployCo，这是一家专注于帮助组织构建和部署 AI 系统的新公司。DeployCo 将派遣 Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) 嵌入客户组织，围绕 AI 重新设计基础设施和工作流程。OpenAI 还同意收购应用 AI 咨询公司 Tomoro，带来约 150 名经验丰富的工程师。该项目获得 TPG、Advent、Bain Capital 等合作伙伴超过 40 亿美元的初始投资。DeployCo 由 OpenAI 控股，旨在加速企业 AI 采纳并确保 AGI 造福人类。
