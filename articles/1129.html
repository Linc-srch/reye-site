Accelerating discovery in India through AI-powered science and education

Accelerating discovery in India through AI-powered science and education

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind announces a national partnership with Indian government bodies and institutions to scale AI in science and education. The collaboration provides access to frontier AI models like AlphaGenome, AI Co-scientist, and Earth AI for scientific research, alongside initiatives to empower students and teachers through Gemini-powered tools, interactive textbooks, and innovation hubs. The partnership also extends to agriculture and energy sectors, leveraging AI for crop productivity and renewable energy forecasting. This effort aims to accelerate discovery, enhance learning, and address national priorities, building on India's leadership in AI adoption and global AI summits.

摘要

Google DeepMind宣布与印度政府及机构建立国家合作伙伴关系，以在科学和教育领域推广AI。合作提供AlphaGenome、AI Co-scientist和Earth AI等前沿模型用于科研，同时通过Gemini驱动的工具、互动教科书和创新中心赋能学生与教师。此外，AI还被应用于农业和能源领域，提升作物产量和可再生能源预测。此举旨在加速发现、改善学习，并应对国家优先事项，依托印度在AI采用和全球AI峰会中的领导地位。
