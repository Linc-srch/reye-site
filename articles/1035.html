Gemini 3.1 Pro: A smarter model for your most complex tasks

Gemini 3.1 Pro: A smarter model for your most complex tasks

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google announces Gemini 3.1 Pro, an upgraded AI model with enhanced reasoning capabilities, achieving a 77.1% score on the ARC-AGI-2 benchmark—more than double its predecessor. Designed for complex tasks beyond simple answers, it supports code-based animation, system synthesis, interactive design, and creative coding. The model rolls out to developers via Gemini API, Google Antigravity, and Android Studio; to enterprises through Vertex AI; and to consumers via the Gemini app and NotebookLM. Released in preview to validate updates and advance agentic workflows, it aims to tackle ambitious challenges across science, engineering, and everyday applications.

摘要

Google 发布 Gemini 3.1 Pro，该升级版 AI 模型在 ARC-AGI-2 基准测试中取得 77.1% 的分数，推理性能较前代翻倍。模型专为复杂任务设计，支持代码动画、系统合成、交互设计与创意编程。开发者可通过 Gemini API、Google Antigravity 及 Android Studio 访问，企业用户通过 Vertex AI，消费者通过 Gemini 应用和 NotebookLM 使用。预览版旨在验证更新并推进 agentic workflows，以应对科学、工程及日常应用中的挑战。
