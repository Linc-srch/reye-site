AutoScout24 scales engineering with AI-powered workflows

AutoScout24 scales engineering with AI-powered workflows

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

AutoScout24, a leading online car marketplace, partnered with OpenAI to transform its engineering workflows using AI-powered tools. By deploying ChatGPT across 2,000 employees and integrating Codex into 1,000 builder roles, the company achieved roughly 10x faster development cycles, reducing project timelines from weeks to days. The dual-layer strategy combined broad AI literacy with deep workflow integration, supported by an AI Champions network. Key results included improved code quality, automated pull request reviews, and expanded innovation capacity for non-technical roles. The initiative demonstrates how large-scale AI adoption can accelerate software delivery while maintaining reliability.

摘要

AutoScout24与OpenAI合作，通过AI驱动的工作流重塑工程流程。公司为2000名员工部署ChatGPT，并将Codex集成到1000个构建者角色中，实现了约10倍的开发周期加速，项目时间从数周缩短至数天。双层策略结合了广泛的AI素养与深度工作流集成，并由AI Champions网络提供支持。关键成果包括代码质量提升、自动化pull request审查以及非技术角色的创新扩展。该案例展示了大规模AI采用如何在保持可靠性的同时加速软件交付。
