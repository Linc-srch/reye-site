Interpretability

Summary

The Interpretability team at Anthropic aims to discover how large language models work internally, serving as a foundation for AI safety. They use mechanistic interpretability techniques such as circuit tracing to understand reasoning processes. Research includes detecting introspection, monitoring persona vectors for traits like sycophancy, and studying superposition. The team comprises researchers from diverse backgrounds including ML, physics, and biology. Their work addresses problems from bias to misuse to autonomous harmful behavior. Recent publications include Natural Language Autoencoders and tools for behavioral differences. This research is critical for ensuring safe and transparent AI systems.

摘要

Anthropic的Interpretability团队致力于揭示大型语言模型的内部工作机制，为AI安全奠定基础。他们运用mechanistic interpretability技术如circuit tracing来理解推理过程。研究包括检测内省、监控sycophancy等persona vectors，以及superposition现象。团队成员背景多样，涵盖机器学习、物理学、生物学等。研究旨在解决偏见、误用和自主有害行为等问题。近期成果包括Natural Language Autoencoders和模型行为差异工具。这些工作对确保AI系统的安全性和透明度至关重要。
