Claude Mythos AI Finds 10,000 High-Severity Flaws in Widely Used Software

Claude Mythos AI Finds 10,000 High-Severity Flaws in Widely Used Software

content-management rlhf supply-chain-attack AI & Machine Learning Vulnerabilities & Exploits
Summary

Anthropic's Project Glasswing, leveraging its Claude Mythos Preview AI model, has uncovered over 10,000 high-severity vulnerabilities in widely used open-source software within the first month of the initiative. Of 1,726 confirmed true positives, 1,094 were rated high or critical, including a critical flaw in WolfSSL (CVE-2026-5194, CVSS 9.1) enabling certificate forgery. The model, provided exclusively to 50 partner organizations, also prevented a $1.5 million fraudulent wire transfer. Anthropic urges faster patch cycles and warns that similar capabilities may soon become broadly available, posing both defensive opportunities and misuse risks. The company has launched a Cyber Verification Program for legitimate security research.

Anthropic的Project Glasswing利用其Claude Mythos Preview AI模型，在一个月内发现了超过10,000个广泛使用的开源软件中的高危漏洞。在1,726个确认的真实阳性中，1,094个被评为高危或严重，包括WolfSSL中的一个关键漏洞（CVE-2026-5194，CVSS 9.1），可导致证书伪造。该模型仅提供给50个合作伙伴，还阻止了一笔150万美元的欺诈电汇。Anthropic敦促加快补丁周期，并警告类似能力可能很快广泛可用，带来防御机会和滥用风险。公司还推出了Cyber Verification Program以支持合法的安全研究。
