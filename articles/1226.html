Societal Impacts

Summary

Anthropic's Societal Impacts team studies how AI is used in the real world, focusing on alignment, economic impact, interpretability, and sociotechnical concerns. They conduct experiments, training methods, and evaluations to understand AI usage, misuse, and future risks. Key studies include a large-scale qualitative survey of 81,000 Claude users, analysis of AI's effect on software development, and a taxonomy of values expressed in real-world language model interactions. The team emphasizes policy-relevant research to inform better AI governance and safety outcomes.

摘要

Anthropic的Societal Impacts团队研究AI在实际世界中的应用，关注对齐、经济影响、可解释性和社会技术问题。他们通过实验、训练方法和评估来理解AI的使用、滥用及未来风险。关键研究包括对81,000名Claude用户的大规模定性调查、AI对软件开发的影响分析，以及实际语言模型交互中价值观的分类。团队强调政策相关的研究，以促进更好的AI治理和安全结果。
