How ChatGPT adoption broadened in early 2026

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

In Q1 2026, ChatGPT consumer adoption broadened across gender, age, and geography. Users with typically feminine names now represent over half of inferred-gender users. Messages from users over 35 gained share, and the fastest-rising countries in per-capita usage included Dominican Republic, Haiti, Japan, and Mexico. Workplace tasks on consumer plans shifted from general content creation toward specialized uses like health documentation and information retrieval. The data indicates ChatGPT is becoming a mainstream tool used by a more diverse user base in more countries for recurring tasks.
