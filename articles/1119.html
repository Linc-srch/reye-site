Gemma 4: Byte for byte, the most capable open models

Gemma 4: Byte for byte, the most capable open models

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind introduces Gemma 4, its most capable open model family, designed for advanced reasoning and agentic workflows. Available in four sizes (E2B, E4B, 26B MoE, 31B Dense), Gemma 4 delivers state-of-the-art intelligence-per-parameter, outperforming models 20x its size on benchmarks. Key features include native function calling, structured JSON output, vision and audio processing, 128K-256K context windows, and support for 140+ languages. Released under Apache 2.0, the models run efficiently on diverse hardware from mobile devices to GPUs. Gemma 4 builds on over 400 million downloads of previous versions, offering developers a powerful open-source foundation for building autonomous agents and AI applications.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布 Gemma 4，其最强大的开放模型系列，专为高级推理和 agentic workflows 设计。提供四种尺寸（E2B、E4B、26B MoE、31B Dense），Gemma 4 在每参数智能上达到领先水平，在基准测试中超越 20 倍规模模型。关键能力包括原生 function calling、结构化 JSON 输出、视觉与音频处理、128K-256K 上下文窗口，以及 140+ 语言支持。采用 Apache 2.0 许可，模型可在从移动设备到 GPU 的多种硬件上高效运行。Gemma 4 基于前代超 4 亿次下载的社区基础，为开发者构建自主 agent 和 AI 应用提供了强大的开源基础。
