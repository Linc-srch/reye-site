How data science teams use Codex

How data science teams use Codex

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

This article from OpenAI Academy explains how data science teams can use Codex to transform scattered inputs—such as dashboards, metric definitions, exports, and business context—into review-ready analysis assets. It details four primary use cases: KPI root-cause analysis, business impact readouts, analytics request handling, and executive KPI reviews. For each use case, the article describes what inputs the team provides, what Codex returns, and how the process works, emphasizing that Codex drafts deliverables so analysts can focus on validating evidence and sharpening recommendations.

摘要

本文来自OpenAI Academy，介绍数据科学团队如何使用Codex将分散的输入（如仪表板、指标定义、导出数据和业务背景）转化为可审查的分析资产。文章详细说明了四个主要用例：KPI根因分析、业务影响报告、分析请求处理和主管KPI评审。每个用例都描述了团队提供什么输入、Codex返回什么输出以及工作流程，强调Codex起草交付物，使分析师能专注于验证证据和优化建议。
