DashAttention: Differentiable and Adaptive Sparse Hierarchical Attention

DashAttention：可微分自适应稀疏分层注意力

Yuxiang Huang, Nuno M. T. Gonçalves, Federico Alvetreti, Lei Li, Xu Han, Edoardo M. Ponti, André F. T. Martins, Marcos V. Treviso · 2026-05-19 · arxiv

Summary

DashAttention replaces the hard top-k block selection in hierarchical attention with differentiable α-entmax routing, enabling adaptive per-query sparsity and end-to-end gradient flow while achieving 75% sparsity with accuracy comparable to full attention.

摘要

本文提出DashAttention，用可微分的α-entmax路由替代分层注意力中硬性的top-k块选择，实现每个查询的自适应稀疏性，同时保持端到端梯度流动。在长上下文任务中，该方法以75%的稀疏性达到与全注意力相当的精度，并实现了最高3.36倍的推理加速。

1) Why-read

DashAttention replaces the hard top-k block selection in hierarchical attention with differentiable α-entmax routing, enabling adaptive per-query sparsity and end-to-end gradient flow while achieving 75% sparsity with accuracy comparable to full attention.

2) CRGP

C — Context

Long-context LLMs face a trade-off between dense softmax attention (which is non-selective and dispersive) and hard sparse routing methods like top-k block selection (which impose a fixed budget and break differentiability). Recent hierarchical attention methods (e.g., NSA, InfLLMv2) use coarse-to-fine block selection but rely on non-differentiable top-k operations, limiting adaptability and gradient flow.

R — Related work

  • NSA (DeepSeek) uses top-k block selection with MLP-based chunk summaries and fine-grained softmax attention on selected blocks.
  • InfLLMv2 uses mean-pooled chunk summaries and top-k routing with a fixed budget per query.
  • MoBA (Mixture of Block Attention) employs mean pooling and top-k routing for long-context efficiency.
  • AdaSplash provides efficient GPU implementations of α-entmax attention for sparse transformers.
  • FlashAttention-3 enables fast softmax attention with memory-efficient tiling.

G — Gap

Existing hierarchical attention methods use a fixed top-k budget for block selection, which assumes all queries require the same number of relevant blocks and prevents gradient flow between the coarse routing stage and fine-grained attention. This limits adaptability to varying information density across queries and heads, and hinders end-to-end optimization of the routing mechanism.

P — Proposal

DashAttention introduces a three-stage differentiable hierarchical attention: (Stage 0) local attention with a learned query vector summarizes each KV chunk; (Stage 1) α-entmax produces an adaptively sparse routing distribution over chunks, with variable support per query; (Stage 2) only the routed chunks are expanded and refined with softmax attention, using a differentiable prior from the router. The entire pipeline is end-to-end trainable and non-dispersive, improving long-context modeling.

4) Experiments

Main results

DashAttention achieves comparable accuracy to full attention with 75% sparsity on long-context tasks. It outperforms NSA and InfLLMv2 on the Pareto frontier of accuracy vs. sparsity, especially in high-sparsity regimes. The Triton implementation achieves up to 3.36× speedup over FlashAttention-3 and 1.35× over InfLLMv2 at inference time.

Ablation

Replacing α-entmax with top-k routing (while keeping other components fixed) degrades performance by 2-3% on long-context benchmarks, confirming the benefit of adaptive differentiable routing. The learned chunk summarization (local attention) outperforms mean pooling by ~1% on average.

Limitations

['The method requires tuning the α hyperparameter in α-entmax, which controls sparsity and may need per-task calibration.', 'The chunk summarization stage adds a small overhead during training, though it is amortized during inference.', 'The approach is evaluated on LLMs up to 7B parameters; scaling to larger models (e.g., 70B+) may reveal additional engineering challenges.']

5) Why it matters

  1. DashAttention provides a principled way to make hierarchical attention fully differentiable, enabling gradient-based optimization of sparsity patterns—relevant for your work on adaptive computation in transformers.
  2. The non-dispersive property of α-entmax directly addresses the long-context degradation issue you've observed in softmax attention, offering a theoretical grounding for sparse attention design.
  3. The efficient Triton implementation with measured speedups makes this approach practical for deployment, aligning with your interest in system-efficient attention mechanisms.

深度笔记

DashAttention：可微分自适应稀疏分层注意力

主要结论 - DashAttention通过α-entmax实现自适应、可微分的稀疏路由，每个查询可动态选择不同数量的KV块。 - 在75%稀疏度下，长上下文任务精度与全注意力相当，优于NSA和InfLLMv2。 - Triton实现相比FlashAttention-3加速最高3.36倍，相比InfLLMv2加速1.35倍。 - 可微分路由使整个流水线端到端可训练，避免了硬top-k的梯度断裂问题。 - 学习到的块摘要（局部注意力）优于平均池化，平均提升约1%。

1) 阅读理由

核心主张：用α-entmax可微分路由替代硬top-k，实现自适应稀疏注意力；关键观察：每个查询所需的相关块数量不同，固定预算限制了模型适应性和梯度优化。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

背景：长上下文LLM面临密集softmax注意力（非选择性、分散）与硬稀疏路由（固定预算、不可微）之间的权衡。现有分层注意力方法（如NSA、InfLLMv2）使用粗到细的块选择，但依赖不可微的top-k操作，限制了适应性和梯度流动。

相关工作：NSA使用基于MLP的块摘要和top-k选择；InfLLMv2使用平均池化摘要和固定预算的top-k路由；MoBA采用平均池化和top-k路由；AdaSplash提供α-entmax的高效GPU实现；FlashAttention-3实现快速softmax注意力。

差距：现有方法使用固定top-k预算，假设所有查询需要相同数量的相关块，阻止了粗路由与细粒度注意力之间的梯度流动，限制了跨查询和跨头的信息密度适应性。

方案：DashAttention引入三阶段可微分分层注意力：阶段0用学习到的查询向量对每个KV块进行局部注意力摘要；阶段1用α-entmax生成自适应稀疏路由分布；阶段2仅对路由到的块进行扩展和softmax注意力，使用来自路由器的可微分先验。整个流水线端到端可训练且非分散。

3) 实验

DashAttention在长上下文任务中以75%稀疏性达到与全注意力相当的精度。在精度-稀疏性帕累托前沿上，它优于NSA和InfLLMv2，尤其是在高稀疏区域。Triton实现推理时相比FlashAttention-3加速最高3.36倍，相比InfLLMv2加速1.35倍。

消融实验

将α-entmax替换为top-k路由（保持其他组件不变）导致长上下文基准性能下降2-3%，证实了自适应可微分路由的优势。学习到的块摘要（局部注意力）平均比平均池化好约1%。

局限性

['该方法需要调整α-entmax中的α超参数，该参数控制稀疏度，可能需要针对不同任务进行校准。', '块摘要阶段在训练时增加少量开销，但推理时可被摊销。', '评估仅在7B参数以内的LLM上进行，扩展到更大模型（如70B+）可能面临额外的工程挑战。']

4) 重要性

  1. DashAttention为分层注意力提供了完全可微分的方案，使稀疏模式可通过梯度优化，对自适应计算研究有直接意义。
  2. α-entmax的非分散特性直接解决了softmax注意力在长上下文中的退化问题，为稀疏注意力设计提供了理论基础。
  3. 高效的Triton实现和实测加速使该方法具备实际部署价值，符合对系统高效注意力机制的需求。
