A new personal finance experience in ChatGPT

A new personal finance experience in ChatGPT

2026-05-19  ·  openai_rss  ·  Original →

Summary

OpenAI has released a preview of a new personal finance experience in ChatGPT for Pro users in the U.S., allowing them to securely connect financial accounts, view a dashboard of spending and portfolio performance, and ask context-aware questions. The feature leverages GPT-5.5's reasoning capabilities to provide personalized insights based on users' financial data, goals, and lifestyle. It supports over 12,000 financial institutions via Plaid, with Intuit integration forthcoming. OpenAI emphasizes user data control and plans to expand to Plus users after learning from early adoption. The tool is designed to complement, not replace, professional financial advice.

摘要

OpenAI向美国Pro用户发布了ChatGPT个人财务体验预览版，用户可安全连接金融账户，查看支出与投资组合仪表盘，并基于GPT-5.5的推理能力提出情境化问题。该功能通过Plaid支持超过12,000家金融机构，即将集成Intuit，强调用户数据控制，计划在早期学习后向Plus用户开放。此工具旨在辅助而非替代专业财务建议。
