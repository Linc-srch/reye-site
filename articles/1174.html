Auditing Privacy in Multi-Tenant RAG under Account Collusion

审计多租户RAG中的账户合谋隐私泄露

content-management rlhf supply-chain-attack

Florian A. D. Burnat, Brittany I. Davidson

启示 · Relevance

This finding directly undermines the privacy guarantees of multi-tenant RAG systems, which are increasingly used to augment autonomous agents; your research should prioritize auditing and red-teaming these systems for collusion-based leakage as a new, high-impact attack vector in agent governance.

该发现直接削弱了多租户RAG系统的隐私保证，而这类系统正越来越多地用于增强自主智能体；您的研究应优先审计和红队测试这些系统在共谋下的信息泄露，将其作为智能体治理中一个高影响力的新型攻击向量。

Summary

Key claim: Per-account DP guarantees in multi-tenant RAG structurally understate joint leakage under account collusion; key observation: the degradation rate is Θ(√k·ε_acc) and is empirically verifiable via a novel audit protocol.

摘要

本文揭示了多租户RAG系统中，多个账户合谋会系统性削弱单账户差分隐私保证，退化率为Θ(√k·ε_acc)。作者设计并验证了首个无需修改部署的审计协议，能够针对检索分数通道发布量化(PASS, ε_audit)判决，填补了理论与实际威胁模型之间的空白。

Deep Note

Deep Note — multitenant-rag-audit

Key Takeaways - Same-index multi-account collusion degrades per-account DP guarantees at a Θ(√k·ε_acc) rate, which is unconditional for Gaussian-noised retrieval. - Cross-tenant and external collusion only match this rate under explicit access-control failure (M4); otherwise they have zero leakage by design. - The paper presents the first audit protocol for unmodified RAG deployments that issues a quantitative (PASS, ε_audit) verdict for the retrieval-score channel without index disclosure or pipeline redesign. - Empirical membership-inference attacks confirm the √k scaling in both scalar and top-K harness settings, with constant-factor degradation in the top-K case. - The audit protocol composes generic cryptographic primitives (Merkle ledgers, ZK proofs, Gaussian noise attestations) with six RAG-specific primitives.

1) Why-read

This paper identifies and quantifies a fundamental privacy leak in multi-tenant RAG under account collusion, and provides the first practical audit protocol to detect it.

2) CRGP

C — Context

Multi-tenant RAG services claim per-account differential privacy to bound leakage from queries to the index. Prior work has focused on single-account DP guarantees or on attacks against centralized DP systems, but the multi-account collusion scenario—where several accounts under the same tenant coordinate—has not been analyzed. Composition theorems suggest that joint leakage grows with the number of colluding accounts, but this has not been empirically validated or made auditable in deployed RAG systems.

R — Related work

  • Differential privacy composition theorems (e.g., Dwork et al., Kairouz et al.) that bound cumulative leakage under multiple queries.
  • Membership inference attacks (MIAs) against DP mechanisms (e.g., Shokri et al., Carlini et al.) that demonstrate practical leakage.
  • Auditing frameworks for DP systems (e.g., Jagielski et al., Nasr et al.) that provide empirical privacy verification.
  • RAG system security analyses (e.g., Liu et al., Zeng et al.) that consider prompt injection or data poisoning but not collusion-based leakage.
  • Cryptographic audit protocols (e.g., Merkle tree-based verifiability, ZK-SNARKs for function application) used in verifiable computation.

G — Gap

Existing privacy analyses for multi-tenant RAG assume per-account DP guarantees are sufficient, ignoring the risk of same-index multi-account collusion. No prior work provides a practical audit protocol that can detect this joint leakage without modifying the RAG pipeline or exposing sensitive index data. The gap is both theoretical (unquantified collusion risk) and practical (no deployable audit mechanism).

P — Proposal

The paper formalizes the same-index multi-account collusion threat model and proves that joint leakage degrades at Θ(√k·ε_acc) for Gaussian-noised retrieval. It then designs an audit protocol that combines cryptographic primitives (Merkle ledgers, ZK proofs, Gaussian noise attestations) with six RAG-specific primitives (embedder commitment, index-content vector commitment, per-account query ledger, noise-then-select attestation, cross-tenant containment proof, coalition-size estimator) to issue a quantitative (PASS, ε_audit) verdict for the retrieval-score channel. The protocol operates against unmodified RAG deployments and supports both closed-form audit bounds and Rényi-DP moments-accountant tracking.

4) Experiments

Main results

Empirical membership-inference AUC against coalition size k: For the scalar mechanism (n=10,000 queries, ε_acc∈{1,2,4}, T=10,000 trials), empirical curves match the predicted 1/2 + 1/2 Φ(√k ε_acc/(4√(log(1/δ_acc)log(1.25/δ_q)))) within one standard error. For the full top-K harness (K=5, 50 background documents, embedding dimension 32, n=200, T=2,000 trials), the √k scaling is preserved with a constant-factor degradation. Real-embedder rate transfer (bge-small-en-v1.5, 384-dim, n=200, T=2,000) shows empirical advantage grows 4.84× across k∈{1,2,5,10,20} against a predicted √20≈4.47. Production-scale HNSW on 10^6 MS MARCO passages (Δ=0.133, T=2,000) shows user-observable hit-indicator AUC is flat (chance), but auditor-observable pooled noisy-score preserves monotone curve at ε_acc∈{8,16}.

Ablation

Alternative-adversary AUC vs. k at ε_acc=4 (n=10^4, T=5,000) shows baseline (blue circles) and Bayes LR (green squares) coincide trial-by-trial (monotonicity identity). Diversified split adversaries at ρ=0.50 (orange triangles) and ρ=0.25 (red triangles) trail baseline at every k>1. No alternative adversary tested exceeds the baseline √k rate. External vs same-tenant collusion against the top-K harness at matched (k, ε_acc) shows curves overlap across the full sweep; absolute AUC differences bounded by 0.022 (median 0.010), within one stderr at T=2,000.

Limitations

['The audit protocol only covers the retrieval-score channel; generation-channel privacy (LLM output conditioned on selected documents) is explicitly scoped out and requires separate auditing.', 'The protocol assumes the RAG deployment is unmodified but requires integration of cryptographic primitives (e.g., Merkle ledgers, ZK proofs) which may introduce computational overhead.', 'Empirical validation is limited to synthetic and small-scale settings (n up to 10^4 queries, embedding dimension 32 or 384); production-scale results (10^6 passages) only show auditor-observable curves, not user-observable attacks.']

5) Why it matters

  1. Directly relevant to your work on privacy auditing in production AI systems—provides a concrete protocol for verifying DP claims in multi-tenant RAG.
  2. Highlights that per-account DP is insufficient under collusion, which is a realistic threat in enterprise multi-tenant deployments.
  3. The cryptographic audit design (Merkle ledgers + ZK proofs) is a practical template for verifiable privacy in other retrieval systems.
  4. The √k scaling result gives a quantitative handle on how many colluding accounts are needed to break a given privacy budget.

6) Next steps

  • [ ] Extend the audit to cover the generation channel by composing with an LLM output audit predicate.
  • [ ] Investigate whether the √k scaling holds for other noise mechanisms (e.g., Laplace) or retrieval algorithms (e.g., approximate nearest neighbor search with different index structures).
  • [ ] Develop a tool that automates the coalition-size estimator and audit verdict generation for continuous monitoring.

1) 阅读理由

核心主张：多租户RAG中的每账户DP保证在账户合谋下系统性低估了联合泄露；关键发现：退化率为Θ(√k·ε_acc)，且可通过新型审计协议进行实证验证。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

背景：生产级RAG服务（如Microsoft 365 Copilot、OpenAI Assistants）为多租户架构，并宣称提供每账户差分隐私。现有成员推断攻击将单一攻击者作为分析单元，但多账户滥用是操作现实。欧盟AI法案等监管框架要求在不暴露模型或数据的情况下进行可审计披露。

相关工作：单攻击者RAG MIA（Naseh2025-riddle等）假设k=1且API无状态；联邦学习合谋（Lyu2023-cerberus等）在训练时针对梯度聚合建模协调；DP-RAG防御（Cheng2025-remoterag）修改部署以减少每次查询泄露，本文保持部署不变；DP组合理论（DworkRothblumVadhan2010等）提供了速率但未涉及威胁模型。

差距：现有每账户DP保证假设单一攻击者（k=1），但生产RAG账户成本低廉，攻击者可汇集多个账户。尚无工作形式化相同索引多账户合谋下的隐私退化，或提供针对未修改部署且无需索引泄露的审计协议。

提案：本文定义了多租户RAG的(ε,δ,k)-合谋DP，并证明了高斯噪声检索下严格的Θ(√k·ε_acc)退化率。设计了验证者可运行的审计协议，使用密码学原语（Merkle账本、ZK证明、高斯噪声证明）和六个RAG特定原语，在不修改部署的情况下为检索分数通道发布(PASS, ε_audit)判决。

3) 实验

主要结果：在基于FAISS的合成多租户测试平台上，实证AUC随k变化的曲线确认了√k趋势：在固定每账户预算下，AUC = 1/2 + Θ(√k·ε_acc)。该趋势在top-K后处理和训练嵌入器下依然成立。通过了两个伪造门测试（斜率门和紧致门）以及一个诊断测试（尺度崩溃）。

消融实验

消融实验确认√k缩放关系在top-K后处理和训练嵌入器下成立；替代攻击者的鲁棒性也得到验证。Rényi-DP矩会计在中等k下比闭式界给出更紧的(ε,δ)对。

局限性

['生成通道隐私（LLM基于选定文档的输出）被明确排除在外，仅审计检索分数通道。', '协议假设提供者诚实遵循承诺阶段；恶意提供者可能在证明前偏离。', '实证验证基于合成部署；真实生产RAG服务可能存在额外复杂性（如缓存、速率限制规避）。']

4) 重要性

  1. 直接针对生产RAG服务操作相关的现实威胁模型（多账户合谋）。
  2. 提供符合监管要求（EU AI法案、DSA）的实用审计协议，无需数据披露即可进行第三方审计。
  3. 通过推导并验证RAG特定的MIA预测，弥合了DP理论与实证MIA之间的差距。
  4. 强调多账户滥用不仅是欺诈问题，更是隐私边界失效，重新定义了操作实践。
