Verizon DBIR: Healthcare Fends Off Increased Social Engineering Attacks

Verizon DBIR: Healthcare Fends Off Increased Social Engineering Attacks

content-management supply-chain-attack zero-trust Vulnerabilities & Exploits Security Operations
Summary

The Verizon Data Breach Investigations Report (DBIR) highlights a significant rise in social engineering attacks targeting the healthcare sector. These attacks, including phishing and pretexting, exploit human vulnerabilities rather than technical flaws. The report emphasizes that healthcare organizations have improved their defenses, but the increasing sophistication of social engineering tactics poses ongoing risks. Key findings include the prevalence of credential theft and the use of ransomware in healthcare breaches. The DBIR underscores the need for continuous employee training and robust security protocols to mitigate these threats.

摘要

Verizon DBIR指出，医疗行业面临的社会工程攻击显著增加，包括钓鱼攻击和借口诈骗。这些攻击利用人为漏洞而非技术缺陷。报告强调，医疗机构虽已加强防御，但攻击手段日益复杂，凭证窃取和勒索软件仍是主要威胁。持续的员工培训和严格的安全协议至关重要。
