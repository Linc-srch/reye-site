AdventHealth advances whole-person care with OpenAI

AdventHealth advances whole-person care with OpenAI

Summary

AdventHealth is leveraging OpenAI's technology to enhance whole-person care by integrating AI into clinical workflows, patient engagement, and operational efficiency. The partnership aims to use large language models to assist healthcare providers with administrative tasks, improve patient communication, and support clinical decision-making while maintaining data privacy and security standards. This initiative represents a significant step in applying advanced AI to holistic healthcare delivery.

摘要

AdventHealth 与 OpenAI 合作，利用大型语言模型（LLM）推进全人护理，通过将 AI 整合到临床工作流、患者沟通和运营管理中，提升效率并支持临床决策，同时确保数据隐私与安全。此举标志着先进 AI 在整体医疗中的关键应用。
