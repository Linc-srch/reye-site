Nano Banana 2: Combining Pro capabilities with lightning-fast speed

Nano Banana 2: Combining Pro capabilities with lightning-fast speed

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind launches Nano Banana 2, a state-of-the-art image generation model that combines the advanced capabilities of Nano Banana Pro with the speed of Gemini Flash. It offers enhanced creative control, subject consistency, precise instruction following, and production-ready specs up to 4K resolution. The model is integrated across Google products including Gemini, Search, Ads, and AI Studio. Google also improves AI content provenance with SynthID and C2PA Content Credentials. Nano Banana 2 aims to make high-quality, rapid image generation accessible to a wider audience.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布 Nano Banana 2，这是最新的图像生成模型，结合了 Nano Banana Pro 的高级能力与 Gemini Flash 的闪电速度。该模型提供增强的创意控制、主体一致性、精确指令遵循以及高达 4K 分辨率的生产级规格。它已集成到 Gemini、Search、Ads 和 AI Studio 等 Google 产品中。同时，Google 通过 SynthID 和 C2PA Content Credentials 改进了 AI 内容溯源技术。Nano Banana 2 旨在让更广泛的用户群体享受高质量、快速的图像生成。
