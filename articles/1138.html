Sea's View on the Future of Agentic Software Development with Codex

Sea's View on the Future of Agentic Software Development with Codex

2026-05-19 · openai_rss

Summary

Sea Limited, a Singapore-based global tech company, is rolling out OpenAI's Codex across its developer organization, with 87% weekly active usage. David Chen, Co-Founder and CPO of Shopee, explains that Codex goes beyond autocomplete to provide deep contextual awareness of large, disparate codebases, enabling engineers to focus on higher-level tasks like architectural design. The company is integrating AI agents into CI/CD pipelines for autonomous testing and debugging. Chen sees this as an organizational paradigm shift, transforming developers into system orchestrators. Sea is also partnering with OpenAI to host a Codex Hackathon Series across Asia to democratize access to AI tools and build a regional AI-native talent ecosystem.

摘要

新加坡全球科技公司Sea Limited正在其开发者组织中推广OpenAI的Codex，周活跃用户率达87%。Shopee联合创始人兼CPO David Chen表示，Codex超越了自动补全功能，能深度理解庞大且分散的代码库上下文，使工程师能专注于架构设计等更高层次的任务。该公司正在将AI代理集成到CI/CD流水线中，用于自主测试和调试。Chen认为这是一场组织范式转变，将开发者转变为系统编排者。Sea还与OpenAI合作，在亚洲举办Codex黑客松系列，以普及AI工具并构建区域性的AI原生人才生态。
