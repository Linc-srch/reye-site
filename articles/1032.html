From games to biology and beyond: 10 years of AlphaGo’s impact

From games to biology and beyond: 10 years of AlphaGo’s impact

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Ten years after AlphaGo's historic victory over Lee Sedol, DeepMind reflects on how the breakthrough catalyzed modern AI and paved the way toward artificial general intelligence (AGI). AlphaGo combined deep neural networks, reinforcement learning, and advanced search to master the complex game of Go, demonstrating AI's potential to tackle real-world scientific problems. This led to AlphaFold 2, which solved the protein folding problem and earned a Nobel Prize, as well as AlphaZero, AlphaProof, and AlphaEvolve. DeepMind now integrates these search and reasoning techniques into Gemini models, aiming to build general AI systems capable of scientific discovery, mathematical reasoning, and algorithmic innovation.

摘要

AlphaGo击败李世石十周年之际，DeepMind回顾了这一突破如何催化现代AI并通向通用人工智能（AGI）。AlphaGo结合深度神经网络、强化学习和高级搜索，攻克了围棋的复杂挑战，展示了AI解决现实科学问题的潜力。这催生了AlphaFold 2（解决蛋白质折叠问题并获诺贝尔奖）、AlphaZero、AlphaProof和AlphaEvolve。DeepMind现将这些搜索与推理技术整合进Gemini模型，旨在构建能进行科学发现、数学推理和算法创新的通用AI系统。
