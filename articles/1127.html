Gemini 3.1 Pro: A smarter model for your most complex tasks

Gemini 3.1 Pro: A smarter model for your most complex tasks

sparse-attention hierarchical-attention rlhf
Summary

Google announces Gemini 3.1 Pro, an upgraded AI model with improved reasoning capabilities for complex tasks. It achieves a 77.1% score on ARC-AGI-2, more than doubling the performance of its predecessor. The model is rolling out to developers via Gemini API, Vertex AI, and Google Antigravity, to enterprises through Vertex AI and Gemini Enterprise, and to consumers via the Gemini app and NotebookLM. It excels in code-based animation, complex system synthesis, interactive design, and creative coding. The preview release aims to validate updates and advance agentic workflows before general availability.

摘要

Google 发布 Gemini 3.1 Pro，这是一款推理能力显著提升的 AI 模型，在 ARC-AGI-2 基准测试中取得 77.1% 的分数，性能较 3 Pro 翻倍。该模型面向开发者通过 Gemini API、Vertex AI 和 Google Antigravity 等平台提供预览，企业用户可通过 Vertex AI 和 Gemini Enterprise 访问，消费者则可通过 Gemini 应用和 NotebookLM 使用。3.1 Pro 在代码动画、复杂系统合成、交互设计和创意编程方面表现出色，旨在验证更新并推进 agentic workflows。
