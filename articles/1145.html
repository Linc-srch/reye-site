AutoScout24 scales engineering with AI-powered workflows

AutoScout24 scales engineering with AI-powered workflows

Summary

AutoScout24 Group, a leading online car marketplace, partnered with OpenAI to scale engineering using AI-powered workflows. By deploying ChatGPT across 2,000 employees and integrating Codex into 1,000 builder roles, the company achieved ~10x faster development cycles, improved code quality, and expanded AI adoption. The dual-layer strategy combined broad AI literacy with deep workflow integration, supported by an AI Champions network. Key results include reduced development timelines from weeks to days, enhanced code consistency, and increased innovation capacity across technical and non-technical teams. The initiative underscores how AI can transform software engineering at scale.

摘要

AutoScout24 Group与OpenAI合作，通过AI驱动的工作流扩展工程能力。该公司向2000名员工部署ChatGPT，并将Codex集成到1000个构建者角色中，实现了约10倍的开发周期加速、代码质量提升和AI采用率扩大。双层策略结合了广泛的AI素养与深度工作流集成，并由AI Champions网络支持。关键成果包括开发时间从数周缩短至数天、代码一致性增强以及跨技术与非技术团队的创新能力提升。该案例展示了AI如何大规模变革软件工程。
