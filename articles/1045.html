Sea's View on the Future of Agentic Software Development with Codex

Sea's View on the Future of Agentic Software Development with Codex

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

Sea Limited, a Singapore-based global tech company, is rolling out OpenAI's Codex across its developer organization to transform software development. David Chen, Co-Founder and CPO of Shopee, explains that Codex goes beyond autocomplete by providing deep contextual awareness of large codebases, enabling engineers to focus on higher-level tasks like architecture and innovation. With 87% weekly active users, Sea is integrating AI agents into CI/CD pipelines for autonomous testing and debugging. Chen views this as an organizational paradigm shift, where developers become system orchestrators. Sea is also hosting a Codex Hackathon Series across Asia to democratize AI tools and build a regional AI-native talent ecosystem.

摘要

Sea Limited正在其开发者组织中推广OpenAI的Codex，以变革软件开发。联合创始人David Chen表示，Codex超越了自动补全，能深度理解大型代码库的上下文，让工程师专注于架构设计等更高层次的任务。Sea拥有87%的周活跃用户，并将AI代理集成到CI/CD流水线中，用于自主测试和调试。Chen认为这是一场组织范式转变，开发者将演变为系统编排者。Sea还在亚洲举办Codex黑客松系列，以普及AI工具并构建区域性的AI原生人才生态。
