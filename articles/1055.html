OpenAI Campus Network: Student club interest form

OpenAI Campus Network: Student club interest form

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI is launching the Campus Network, a global initiative partnering with university student clubs to foster AI-native campuses. Through this interest form, clubs can apply for hands-on AI learning support, early access to tools like Codex, event resources, and connections with a worldwide network of student leaders. The program targets diverse club types—from AI/ML to entrepreneurship—and offers opportunities such as workshops, research collaborations, ambassador programs, and career development. By collecting details on club focus, activity level, and AI usage, OpenAI aims to tailor support and build a community shaping the future of learning and work.

摘要

OpenAI 推出 Campus Network 全球计划，与大学学生俱乐部合作打造 AI-native 校园。通过此兴趣表单，俱乐部可申请 hands-on AI 学习支持、Codex 等工具早期访问、活动资源及全球学生领袖网络连接。项目面向 AI/ML、创业等多元俱乐部类型，提供 workshops、研究合作、ambassador 项目及职业发展机会。OpenAI 通过收集俱乐部焦点、活跃度及 AI 使用情况，定制支持以构建塑造学习与工作未来的社区。
