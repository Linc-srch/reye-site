How data science teams use Codex

2026-05-19 · openai_rss

Summary

This article from OpenAI Academy explains how data science teams can leverage Codex to transform scattered inputs—such as dashboards, metric definitions, exports, and business context—into polished analysis assets. It outlines four key use cases: KPI root-cause analysis, business impact readouts, analytics request handling, and executive KPI reviews. For each use case, it details what the team provides and what Codex returns, including charts, caveats, source links, and recommendations. The goal is to accelerate the creation of first drafts, allowing analysts to focus on validation and decision-making. The article includes starter prompts and real-world examples to guide adoption.
