Lyria 3 Pro: Create longer tracks in more

Lyria 3 Pro: Create longer tracks in more

2026-05-19  ·  deepmind_blog  ·  Original →

Summary

Google DeepMind announces Lyria 3 Pro, an advanced music generation model that creates tracks up to 3 minutes long with structural awareness of intros, verses, choruses, and bridges. It is integrated into Vertex AI, Google AI Studio, Gemini API, Google Vids, the Gemini app, and ProducerAI. The model is developed responsibly with musician partnerships, uses SynthID watermarking, and avoids mimicking existing artists. Lyria 3 Pro aims to scale high-fidelity music production for developers, businesses, and creators.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布 Lyria 3 Pro，这是先进的音乐生成模型，可创建长达 3 分钟、具有结构感知（如主歌、副歌）的曲目。该模型已集成到 Vertex AI、Google AI Studio、Gemini API、Google Vids、Gemini 应用和 ProducerAI 中。开发过程与音乐人合作，采用 SynthID 水印，并避免模仿现有艺术家，旨在为开发者、企业和创作者规模化高质量音乐制作。
