How enterprises are scaling AI

How enterprises are scaling AI

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

This guide synthesizes insights from European enterprise leaders at Philips, BBVA, and others, revealing that scaling AI is less about technology deployment and more about building trust, adoption, and iterative improvement. Key patterns include prioritizing culture over tooling, using governance as an enabler, fostering ownership over consumption, ensuring quality before scale, and protecting judgment work through hybrid workflows. The report emphasizes that sustained impact requires treating AI as an operating layer and leadership discipline, with human oversight embedded in end-to-end workflows.

摘要

本指南汇集了Philips、BBVA等欧洲企业领导者的见解，指出扩展AI的关键不在于技术部署，而在于建立信任、采用和持续改进。核心模式包括：文化优先于工具、将治理作为赋能因素、鼓励所有权而非消费、质量优先于规模，以及通过混合工作流保护判断工作。报告强调，持久影响需要将AI视为运营层和领导力学科，并在端到端工作流中嵌入人工监督。
