对于 Harmony Security 和 SOC 研究人员而言，这份简报提供了 AI 被武器化用于入侵（SHADOW-AETHER 活动）的关键现实证据，验证了先进 AI/Agent 安全防御和端侧威胁缓解的必要性。OrBit rootkit 的演变展示了对 Linux 终端的持续威胁，与 OS 安全对标相关。NCSC 关于 Agentic AI 的指南强调了保护自主智能体工作流的紧迫性，这是两个研究领域的核心关注点。

Summary

The ThreatsDay bulletin reports on a surge in AI-driven intrusions targeting Latin American governments, the discovery of 47 zero-days at Pwn2Own Berlin 2026, and the continued evolution of the OrBit Linux rootkit, alongside other notable security events like a Composer token leak and new NCSC guidance on agentic AI security.

摘要

ThreatsDay 简报报道了针对拉丁美洲政府的 AI 驱动入侵激增、2026 年 Pwn2Own 柏林大赛中发现 47 个零日漏洞、以及 OrBit Linux rootkit 的持续演变，同时还包括 Composer 令牌泄露和 NCSC 关于 Agentic AI 安全的新指南等其他值得注意的安全事件。

Deep Note

Key Points

  • AI-Driven Intrusions (SHADOW-AETHER): Two campaigns (SHADOW-AETHER-040 and -064) used agentic AI to dynamically generate hacking tools and establish tunnels into victim networks, bypassing signature-based detection.
  • Pwn2Own Berlin 2026: 47 zero-days were exploited, with DEVCORE winning $505,000 for hacking Microsoft SharePoint, Exchange, Edge, and Windows 11.
  • OrBit Linux Rootkit: The userland rootkit is still active, with two lineages (full-featured and lite) being refined, including new evasion and PAM impersonation techniques. It is linked to the Embargo ransomware group (Blockade Spider).
  • NCSC Agentic AI Guidance: The UK NCSC released guidance emphasizing the risk of over-privileged or poorly designed AI agents leading to serious incidents.
  • Composer Token Leak (CVE-2026-45793): A vulnerability in Composer (PHP) leaks GitHub Actions tokens to logs due to a format change.
  • Other Events: Poland urges switch from Signal to mSzyfr; Dutch police unmask 74 fraud suspects via public billboards; Gunra ransomware targets South Korea; Trump acknowledges US cyber espionage against China.

Context & Contrast

This bulletin contrasts with prior periods by highlighting a shift from traditional malware to AI-generated, dynamic attack tools that evade signature-based defenses. The OrBit rootkit's continued evolution over 4 years shows a long-term, adaptive threat, unlike many one-off malware strains. The NCSC guidance is a direct response to the rapid enterprise adoption of agentic AI, marking a move from theoretical risk to actionable security policy.

Why It Matters

The SHADOW-AETHER campaigns are a landmark demonstration of AI agents being used for active, real-world intrusions, not just reconnaissance. This validates the threat model for AI-powered attacks and underscores the need for AI-driven defenses (AI4SEC). The OrBit rootkit's persistence and evolution highlight the ongoing challenge of securing Linux endpoints, especially in edge and cloud environments. The NCSC guidance provides a regulatory and best-practice framework that will shape enterprise AI security policies globally.

Critical Take

While the SHADOW-AETHER campaigns are significant, the report does not provide deep technical details on the specific AI models or agent architectures used. The claim of 'bypassing AI safety controls' by framing requests as pen-testing is a known weakness, but the exact method is not fully disclosed. The OrBit analysis, while detailed, is based on artifacts and not a live incident response, so the full scope of its current deployment is unclear. The NCSC guidance is high-level and lacks specific implementation details.

What to Watch

  1. SHADOW-AETHER Evolution: Monitor for similar AI-driven intrusion campaigns targeting other regions or sectors. Look for disclosure of the specific AI models and agent frameworks used.
  2. OrBit Lineage Divergence: Track the development of the two OrBit lineages, especially the 'lite' version, and any new evasion techniques targeting auditd or PAM.
  3. NCSC Guidance Adoption: Watch for how enterprises implement the NCSC's agentic AI security principles and if other national cybersecurity agencies release similar guidance.
  4. Composer Ecosystem Impact: Monitor for follow-up vulnerabilities in the PHP/Composer supply chain and the adoption of the patched versions.
  5. Pwn2Own Exploit Disclosures: Track the disclosure and patching of the 47 zero-days, particularly the Windows 11 and Microsoft Exchange exploits.
深度笔记

要点

  • AI 驱动入侵 (SHADOW-AETHER)： 两个活动（SHADOW-AETHER-040 和 -064）使用 Agentic AI 动态生成黑客工具并建立进入受害者网络的隧道，绕过了基于签名的检测。
  • Pwn2Own 柏林 2026： 47 个零日漏洞被利用，DEVCORE 因入侵 Microsoft SharePoint、Exchange、Edge 和 Windows 11 赢得 50.5 万美元。
  • OrBit Linux Rootkit： 用户态 rootkit 仍然活跃，有两个分支（功能完整版和精简版）正在被改进，包括新的规避和 PAM 模拟技术。它与 Embargo 勒索软件团伙（Blockade Spider）有关联。
  • NCSC Agentic AI 指南： 英国 NCSC 发布指南，强调权限过高或设计不佳的 AI Agent 可能导致严重事件的风险。
  • Composer 令牌泄露 (CVE-2026-45793)： Composer（PHP）中的一个漏洞因格式更改将 GitHub Actions 令牌泄露到日志中。
  • 其他事件： 波兰敦促从 Signal 切换到 mSzyfr；荷兰警方通过公共广告牌识别出 74 名诈骗嫌疑人；Gunra 勒索软件针对韩国；特朗普承认美国对中国的网络间谍活动。

背景与对比

这份简报与以往时期形成对比，突出了从传统恶意软件向 AI 生成的、动态攻击工具的转变，这些工具能够规避基于签名的防御。OrBit rootkit 持续 4 年的演变展示了一种长期、自适应的威胁，不同于许多一次性的恶意软件变种。NCSC 的指南是对企业快速采用 Agentic AI 的直接回应，标志着从理论风险向可操作安全策略的转变。

重要性

SHADOW-AETHER 活动是 AI Agent 被用于主动、真实世界入侵（而不仅仅是侦察）的标志性演示。这验证了 AI 驱动攻击的威胁模型，并强调了 AI 驱动防御（AI4SEC）的必要性。OrBit rootkit 的持续存在和演变突显了保护 Linux 终端（尤其是在边缘和云环境中）的持续挑战。NCSC 指南提供了一个监管和最佳实践框架，将塑造全球的企业 AI 安全政策。

批判性视角

虽然 SHADOW-AETHER 活动意义重大，但报告未提供所使用的特定 AI 模型或 Agent 架构的深入技术细节。将请求伪装成渗透测试以“绕过 AI 安全控制”的说法是一个已知的弱点，但确切方法未完全披露。OrBit 分析虽然详细，但基于工件而非实时事件响应，因此其当前部署的全部范围尚不清楚。NCSC 指南是高层级的，缺乏具体的实施细节。

后续关注

  1. SHADOW-AETHER 演变： 监控针对其他地区或行业的类似 AI 驱动入侵活动。关注所使用的特定 AI 模型和 Agent 框架的披露。
  2. OrBit 分支分化： 跟踪两个 OrBit 分支（尤其是“精简版”）的发展，以及任何针对 auditd 或 PAM 的新规避技术。
  3. NCSC 指南采纳： 观察企业如何实施 NCSC 的 Agentic AI 安全原则，以及其他国家网络安全机构是否发布类似指南。
  4. Composer 生态影响： 监控 PHP/Composer 供应链中的后续漏洞以及补丁版本的采用情况。
  5. Pwn2Own 漏洞披露： 跟踪 47 个零日漏洞的披露和修补情况，特别是 Windows 11 和 Microsoft Exchange 的漏洞。
