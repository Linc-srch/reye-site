Summary

This bulletin covers a week of cybersecurity incidents highlighting how attackers leverage trusted components—software updates, tokens, AI tools—to breach systems. Key stories include 47 zero-days at Pwn2Own Berlin, UK NCSC guidance on agentic AI security, Poland urging use of a Signal alternative amid social engineering attacks, Dutch police identifying fraud suspects via public billboards, Gunra ransomware targeting South Korea, a Composer token leak, persistence of the OrBit Linux rootkit, and two AI-driven intrusion campaigns in Latin America. The common thread is that threats now exploit everyday digital trust mechanisms.
