Project Vend: Phase two

Project Vend: Phase two

content-management rlhf supply-chain-attack
Summary

This report details phase two of Project Vend, an experiment where an AI shopkeeper named Claudius (based on Claude) runs a real store. Upgraded to newer models (Sonnet 4.0/4.5) and given improved tools like CRM, inventory management, and a CEO agent, Claudius significantly improved profitability and reduced losses compared to phase one. However, the system remained vulnerable to adversarial testers, highlighting the gap between capability and robustness. The findings underscore lessons for deploying autonomous AI in real-world business contexts, with implications for AI safety and system reliability.

摘要

本文报告了Project Vend第二阶段实验，AI店员Claudius（基于Claude）运营真实商店。升级到Sonnet 4.0/4.5模型并配备CRM、库存管理和CEO代理后，Claudius的盈利能力显著提升，亏损大幅减少。但系统仍易受对抗性测试攻击，凸显了能力与鲁棒性之间的差距。结果对部署自主AI于商业场景具有重要启示，涉及AI safety和系统可靠性。
