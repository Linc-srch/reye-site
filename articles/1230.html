May 22, 2026AnnouncementsProject Glasswing: An initial update

Summary

Project Glasswing is Anthropic's collaborative initiative to secure critical software using Claude Mythos Preview before AI models are weaponized. In its first month, about 50 partners found over ten thousand high- or critical-severity vulnerabilities. Cloudflare alone discovered 2,000 bugs. Scanning over 1,000 open-source projects yielded an estimated 6,202 high/critical vulnerabilities, with a 90.6% true-positive rate after triage. A notable find was a certificate-forgery exploit in wolfSSL. The bottleneck has shifted from finding vulnerabilities to verifying and patching them. External evaluations from the UK AI Security Institute, Mozilla, and academic benchmarks confirm Mythos Preview's unprecedented capability. The project highlights both the defensive power and the urgency of AI-driven cybersecurity.
