May 14, 2026Policy2028: Two scenarios for global AI leadership

supply-chain-attack
Summary

This policy paper outlines two scenarios for global AI leadership by 2028, focusing on US-China competition. It argues that democratic nations must maintain a lead in compute and model intelligence to prevent authoritarian regimes like the CCP from shaping AI norms. The first scenario envisions tightened export controls and disrupted distillation attacks securing a democratic advantage. The second warns of an equal or overtaken frontier if inaction allows loopholes. The paper emphasizes the urgency of policy action to avoid automated repression and preserve safety, while noting that a lead could enable constructive engagement with China on AI governance.

摘要

本文提出了2028年全球AI领导权的两种情景，聚焦中美竞争。文章认为民主国家必须在compute和模型智能方面保持领先，以防止CCP等威权政权塑造AI规范。第一种情景通过加强export controls和阻断distillation attacks来确保民主优势；第二种情景因不作为导致中国接近或超越前沿。强调政策紧迫性以避免自动化压制，并指出领先地位可促进与中国的AI安全合作。
