Drupal Core SQL Injection Bug Actively Exploited, Added to CISA KEV

Drupal Core SQL Injection Bug Actively Exploited, Added to CISA KEV

supply-chain-attack
Summary

The U.S. CISA has added a critical Drupal Core SQL injection vulnerability (CVE-2026-9082, CVSS 6.5) to its Known Exploited Vulnerabilities catalog after evidence of active exploitation. The bug affects all supported Drupal versions and can lead to privilege escalation and remote code execution. Over 15,000 attack attempts have been observed targeting nearly 6,000 sites across 65 countries, primarily gaming and financial services. While most activity appears to be probing, the risk of data extraction or privilege escalation is high. Patches are available, and U.S. federal agencies must apply fixes by May 27, 2026.
