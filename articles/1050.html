This article from OpenAI Academy explains how finance teams can leverage Codex to automate and streamline key workflows such as monthly business reviews, financial model cleanup, recurring CFO/board reporting packs, and variance driver analysis. Codex ingests existing workbooks, dashboards, and notes to produce review-ready outputs without requiring coding skills. Each use case includes a starter prompt, suggested plugins, and a real-world example. The goal is to reduce time spent on initial drafting, allowing teams to focus on analysis, validation, and strategic decision-making.
