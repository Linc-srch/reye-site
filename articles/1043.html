A new personal finance experience in ChatGPT

A new personal finance experience in ChatGPT

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI is releasing a preview of a new personal finance experience in ChatGPT for Pro users in the U.S., allowing them to securely connect financial accounts via Plaid, view a dashboard of spending and portfolio performance, and ask context-aware questions. The feature leverages GPT-5.5's reasoning capabilities to provide personalized insights, such as saving plans based on real transaction data. Over 200 million monthly users already seek financial advice from ChatGPT. The rollout starts with a limited group to refine the experience before expanding to Plus and free tiers. OpenAI emphasizes that this tool is not a substitute for professional financial advice.

摘要

OpenAI 向美国 Pro 用户推出 ChatGPT 个人财务功能预览，支持通过 Plaid 安全连接银行账户，展示支出与投资仪表盘，并基于 GPT-5.5 的推理能力提供个性化财务建议（如储蓄计划）。每月超 2 亿用户已用 ChatGPT 处理财务问题。该功能先在小范围测试，后续将扩展至 Plus 和免费用户。OpenAI 强调此工具不能替代专业财务顾问。
