Summary

This article from OpenAI Academy explains how business operations teams can leverage Codex to streamline their workflow. By aggregating context from scattered sources like project trackers, KPI dashboards, and stakeholder notes, Codex generates first-draft artifacts such as off-track briefs, strategic initiative updates, leadership decision packets, and progress reports. The team retains judgment and recommendations, while Codex accelerates the creation of working drafts. The article provides specific use cases with starter prompts and real-world examples, emphasizing how Codex helps turn operational context into actionable assets for review and decision-making.
