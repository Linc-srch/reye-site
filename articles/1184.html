Simulate real-world places with Project Genie and Street View

Simulate real-world places with Project Genie and Street View

content-management digital-publishing hierarchical-attention
Summary

Google DeepMind and Google Labs announce Project Genie, a general-purpose world model now integrated with Street View imagery to generate interactive, real-world-grounded virtual environments. Users can select a U.S. location, apply creative styles like 'Ocean World' or 'B&W film', and describe a character to create imaginative scenes tied to actual places. The capability, powered by Maps Imagery Grounding, is available to Google AI Ultra subscribers globally. Project Genie remains an experimental research prototype, with plans to expand location coverage and improve detail accuracy.

摘要

Google DeepMind与Google Labs推出Project Genie，这是一个通用世界模型，现整合Street View图像，可生成基于真实地点的交互式虚拟环境。用户可选择美国地点，应用如“Ocean World”或“B&W film”等创意风格，并描述角色，从而创建与现实场景关联的想象世界。该功能由Maps Imagery Grounding技术驱动，向全球Google AI Ultra订阅用户开放。Project Genie仍为实验性研究原型，计划扩展地点覆盖并提升细节精度。
