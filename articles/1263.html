Akamai Joins Growing Chorus of Vendors Betting Big on Secure Enterprise Browsers

Akamai Joins Growing Chorus of Vendors Betting Big on Secure Enterprise Browsers

Summary

Akamai's acquisition of LayerX for $205 million underscores the growing trend of secure enterprise browsers as critical components for Zero Trust Network Access (ZTNA) and SaaS security. LayerX's browser extension enforces security policies on major browsers like Chrome and Firefox, monitoring actions before encryption. Akamai plans to integrate LayerX into its ZTNA portfolio, extending access control into browser sessions. This move follows similar acquisitions by Zscaler and CrowdStrike, reflecting the industry's shift toward endpoint-level browser security. Gartner predicts 25% of organizations will adopt secure enterprise browsers by 2028, driven by AI adoption and remote work.
