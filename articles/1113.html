Enabling a new model for healthcare with AI co-clinician

Enabling a new model for healthcare with AI co-clinician

Summary

Google DeepMind introduces the AI co-clinician research initiative to address the global shortage of healthcare workers by augmenting clinicians with collaborative AI agents under expert supervision. The system is evaluated in both clinician-facing and patient-facing settings. In clinician-facing tests, it outperforms leading evidence synthesis tools on 98 primary care queries with zero critical errors in 97 cases, and surpasses frontier models on open-ended medication queries (OpenFDA RxQA). In patient-facing telemedical simulations using real-time multimodal AI (audio/video), it demonstrates capabilities like guiding physical exams and correcting inhaler technique, though expert physicians still outperform it overall, suggesting AI is best used as a supportive tool.

摘要

Google DeepMind 宣布了 AI co-clinician 研究计划，旨在通过协作式 AI 代理在专家临床监督下辅助医生，应对全球医疗人员短缺。在面向医生的评估中，该系统在 98 个初级保健查询上优于领先的证据合成工具，97 例无严重错误，并在开放式药物问答（OpenFDA RxQA）上超越前沿模型。在面向患者的远程医疗模拟中，利用实时多模态 AI（音频/视频）展示了指导体检和纠正吸入器使用等能力，但专家医生整体表现更优，表明 AI 当前最适合作为支持工具而非替代品。
