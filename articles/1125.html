Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite: Built for intelligence at scale

Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite: Built for intelligence at scale

Summary

Google has launched Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite, its fastest and most cost-efficient AI model, now available in preview via the Gemini API and Vertex AI. Priced at $0.25/1M input tokens and $1.50/1M output tokens, it offers 2.5x faster time to first token and 45% higher output speed than 2.5 Flash while maintaining quality. It achieves a 1432 Elo score on Arena.ai and excels on benchmarks like GPQA Diamond (86.9%) and MMMU Pro (76.8%). The model supports adjustable thinking levels for high-volume tasks such as translation, content moderation, UI generation, and simulations, with early adopters including Latitude, Cartwheel, and Whering.

摘要

Google 发布了 Gemini 3.1 Flash-Lite，这是其最快且最具成本效益的 AI 模型，现通过 Gemini API 和 Vertex AI 提供预览。定价为每百万输入 tokens $0.25、每百万输出 tokens $1.50，相比 2.5 Flash，首次输出 token 速度提升 2.5 倍，输出速度提升 45%，同时保持相似或更优质量。该模型在 Arena.ai 上获得 1432 Elo 评分，在 GPQA Diamond 和 MMMU Pro 等基准测试中表现优异。它支持可调节的思考层级，适用于翻译、内容审核、UI 生成和模拟等高容量任务，早期采用者包括 Latitude、Cartwheel 和 Whering。
