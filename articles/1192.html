Advancing content provenance for a safer, more transparent AI ecosystem

Advancing content provenance for a safer, more transparent AI ecosystem

Summary

This paper discusses the importance of content provenance in the AI ecosystem, focusing on methods to track and verify the origin and history of digital content to enhance transparency and safety. It explores technical approaches such as cryptographic signatures and watermarking to establish trust in AI-generated media, aiming to mitigate risks like misinformation and deepfakes. The work emphasizes the need for standardized provenance frameworks to support accountability and ethical AI deployment.

摘要

本文探讨了内容溯源在AI生态系统中的重要性，重点介绍了通过加密签名和水印等技术手段追踪和验证数字内容来源与历史的方法，以增强透明度和安全性。研究旨在建立标准化的溯源框架，应对虚假信息和deepfake等风险，支持AI的负责任部署。
