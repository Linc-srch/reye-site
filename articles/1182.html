Making it easier to understand how content was created and edited

Making it easier to understand how content was created and edited

2026-05-20 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google is expanding its content transparency and verification tools across Search, Gemini, Chrome, Pixel, and Cloud to help users understand the origin and edits of digital content. Key technologies include SynthID, a digital watermark for AI-generated media, and C2PA Content Credentials, an industry standard for tracking content provenance. Pixel phones now support Content Credentials for camera-captured images and soon video. Google also introduces an AI Content Detection API on Cloud and partners with OpenAI, Kakao, ElevenLabs, Meta, and NVIDIA to promote interoperable provenance standards. The goal is to empower users to verify whether content is AI-generated, authentic, or altered.

摘要

Google正在扩展其内容透明度和验证工具，覆盖Search、Gemini、Chrome、Pixel和Cloud，帮助用户了解数字内容的来源和编辑历史。核心技术包括SynthID（用于AI生成内容的数字水印）和C2PA Content Credentials（追踪内容来源的行业标准）。Pixel手机已支持为相机拍摄的图像添加Content Credentials，即将支持视频。Google还推出基于Cloud的AI Content Detection API，并与OpenAI、Kakao、ElevenLabs、Meta和NVIDIA合作推广互操作标准，旨在让用户轻松验证内容是否为AI生成、真实或经过修改。
