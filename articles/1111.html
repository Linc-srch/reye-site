Reimagining the mouse pointer for the AI era

Reimagining the mouse pointer for the AI era

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind is reimagining the mouse pointer for the AI era by integrating Gemini-powered capabilities that understand both visual and semantic context. The goal is to eliminate the need for users to drag information into separate AI tools, instead enabling intuitive, cross-application collaboration through pointing and speaking. Four principles guide this work: maintaining user flow, combining pointing with natural language, leveraging contextual shorthand like 'this' and 'that', and turning pixels into actionable entities. Experimental demos show users editing images, finding map locations, or summarizing PDFs simply by pointing. The technology is being integrated into Chrome and the Googlebook laptop experience.

摘要

Google DeepMind 正在重新构想 AI 时代的鼠标指针，通过集成 Gemini 使其理解视觉和语义上下文。目标是消除用户将信息拖入独立 AI 工具的繁琐操作，实现跨应用的直观协作。四项原则指导这一工作：保持用户流程、结合指向与自然语言、利用“这个”和“那个”等上下文简写、将像素转化为可操作实体。实验演示显示，用户只需指向即可编辑图像、查找地图位置或总结 PDF。该技术正被集成到 Chrome 和 Googlebook 笔记本体验中。
