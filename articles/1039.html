OpenAI and Malta partner to bring ChatGPT Plus to all citizens

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI and the Government of Malta have announced a pioneering partnership to provide ChatGPT Plus to all Maltese citizens, marking the first national-scale initiative of its kind. The program includes an AI literacy course developed by the University of Malta, designed to teach citizens about AI capabilities and responsible use. Upon completion, participants receive free access to ChatGPT Plus for one year. The initiative aims to make intelligence a global utility, ensuring equitable access to advanced AI tools. Malta's Minister for Economy and OpenAI's George Osborne emphasized the importance of national AI adoption, with Malta leading Europe in this strategic effort to empower citizens and boost the economy.
