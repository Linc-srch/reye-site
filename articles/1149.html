How enterprises are scaling AI

How enterprises are scaling AI

2026-05-19 · openai_rss

Summary

This guide synthesizes insights from European enterprise leaders at Philips, BBVA, Mirakl, Scout24, Jetbrains, and Scania, revealing that scaling AI is less about technical rollout and more about building trust, adoption, and continuous improvement. Key patterns include prioritizing culture over tooling, using governance as an enabler, fostering ownership over consumption, ensuring quality before scale, and protecting judgment work through hybrid workflows. The report emphasizes that sustained impact requires trust, ownership, and quality built in from the start, with AI embedded in end-to-end workflows under human oversight.

摘要

本指南综合了Philips、BBVA、Mirakl、Scout24、Jetbrains和Scania等欧洲企业领导者的见解，指出规模化AI的关键不在于技术部署，而在于建立信任、采用和持续改进的条件。核心模式包括：文化优先于工具、将治理作为赋能因素、鼓励所有权而非消费、质量优先于规模，以及通过混合工作流保护判断工作。报告强调，持久的影响力需要从一开始就建立信任、所有权和质量，将AI嵌入端到端工作流并保持人类监督。
