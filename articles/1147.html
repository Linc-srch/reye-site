Summary

In Q1 2026, ChatGPT consumer adoption broadened significantly across gender, age, and geography. Users with typically feminine names now represent over half of inferred-gender users, and those over 35 increased their message share. Adoption rose fastest in Latin America, Asia-Pacific, and Africa, with countries like the Dominican Republic and Japan climbing the per-capita ranking. Workplace use on consumer plans shifted from general content creation toward specialized tasks like health documentation and information retrieval. The data indicates ChatGPT is transitioning from early adopter tool to mainstream utility, with more diverse users and deeper integration into recurring workflows.
