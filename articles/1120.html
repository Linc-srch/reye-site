Gemini 3.1 Flash Live: Making audio AI more natural and reliable

Gemini 3.1 Flash Live: Making audio AI more natural and reliable

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google has launched Gemini 3.1 Flash Live, its highest-quality audio model designed for natural and reliable real-time dialogue. The model offers improved precision, lower latency, and better tonal understanding, enabling fluid voice interactions. Developers can access it via the Gemini Live API in Google AI Studio, while enterprises can use it for customer experience. The model leads benchmarks like ComplexFuncBench Audio and Scale AI's Audio MultiChallenge. It is available in over 200 countries through Search Live and Gemini Live, and all audio outputs are watermarked with SynthID to prevent misinformation.

摘要

Google 发布了 Gemini 3.1 Flash Live，这是其最高质量的音频模型，旨在实现自然且可靠的实时对话。该模型具有更高的精度、更低的延迟和更好的语调理解能力，支持流畅的语音交互。开发者可通过 Google AI Studio 中的 Gemini Live API 访问，企业可用于客户体验。该模型在 ComplexFuncBench Audio 和 Scale AI 的 Audio MultiChallenge 等基准测试中领先，并已在超过200个国家通过 Search Live 和 Gemini Live 提供，所有音频输出均使用 SynthID 水印以防止 misinformation。
