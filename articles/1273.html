Processes & Culture Top Reasons Behind Data Breaches

Processes & Culture Top Reasons Behind Data Breaches

content-management supply-chain-attack zero-trust Governance & Policy Security Operations
Summary

This article highlights that data breaches are primarily caused by inadequate processes and weak security culture rather than technical failures. It emphasizes that human factors, such as poor compliance with protocols and lack of awareness, are the leading contributors to security incidents. The piece calls for organizations to prioritize governance, training, and cultural change to reduce risk.

摘要

本文指出，数据泄露的主要原因并非技术缺陷，而是流程不完善和安全文化薄弱。人为因素，如对协议遵守不力及缺乏安全意识，是安全事件的主要诱因。文章呼吁组织优先加强治理、培训和文化变革以降低风险。
