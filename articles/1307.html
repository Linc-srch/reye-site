Thaka at KSAA-2026 Task 2: Regularized Fine-Tuning for Arabic Speech Diacritization

Thaka在KSAA-2026任务2中的表现：面向阿拉伯语音节标注的正则化微调

sparse-attention hierarchical-attention rlhf AI & Machine Learning

Meshal Alamr, Hassan Alqaeri, Abdullah Aldahlawi

Summary

This paper shows that aggressive training regularization and inference-time ensembling can achieve state-of-the-art Arabic speech diacritization with very limited data, outperforming architectural changes.

摘要

本文展示了在极低资源条件下，通过激进的正则化训练和推理时集成，可以在阿拉伯语音节标注任务上取得最先进的结果。仅使用2327个训练样本且无外部数据，系统实现了23.26%的词错误率（WER），在KSAA-2026共享任务中排名第一。

Deep Note

Deep Note — thaka-ksaa2026

Key Takeaways - Winning system for KSAA-2026 Task 2 achieves 23.26% WER using only 2,327 training samples with no external data. - Training regularization (R-Drop, Focal Loss, high weight decay) yields 3.25 pp WER gain over standard fine-tuning. - MC Dropout ensembling (200 stochastic passes across 4 checkpoints) adds 1.16 pp improvement. - At low data scale, optimization strategy matters more than architectural modifications. - Multimodal fusion of frozen Whisper speech encoder with CATT text encoder is effective for Arabic diacritization.

1) Why-read

This paper shows that aggressive training regularization and inference-time ensembling can achieve state-of-the-art Arabic speech diacritization with very limited data, outperforming architectural changes.

2) CRGP

C — Context

Arabic diacritization is critical for TTS, MT, and language learning but remains challenging due to ambiguity, especially in dialectal Arabic. Speech signals provide complementary disambiguation cues. The KSAA-2026 shared task provides a low-resource benchmark with only 2,327 training samples and no external data allowed.

R — Related work

  • CATT-Whisper (Ghannam et al., 2025): multimodal architecture combining CATT text encoder with Whisper speech encoder, won NADI 2025 dialectal diacritization track.
  • R-Drop (Liang et al., 2021): consistency regularization penalizing divergent predictions between two dropout-masked forward passes.
  • MC Dropout (Gal and Ghahramani, 2016): ensemble-like inference by keeping dropout active at test time.
  • AraBERT (Antoun et al., 2020) and CATT (Alasmary et al., 2024): pretrained encoders for Arabic diacritization.
  • Optuna (Akiba et al., 2019): hyperparameter optimization framework used to tune the training recipe.

G — Gap

Previous work on Arabic diacritization either relied on large datasets or focused on text-only models, which struggle with ambiguity. The KSAA-2026 task presents an extreme low-resource multimodal setting where standard fine-tuning of CATT-Whisper yields only 30.43% WER. The specific limitation is how to maximize performance with only 2,187 effective training samples and no external data.

P — Proposal

The authors fine-tune CATT-Whisper with a heavily regularized training recipe: R-Drop consistency loss, Focal Loss with label smoothing, high weight decay (0.098), and Optuna-optimized hyperparameters. At inference, they average softmax probabilities from 200 stochastic forward passes (4 checkpoints × 50 MC Dropout passes). This achieves 23.26% WER, placing 1st in the shared task.

---

4) Experiments

Main results

Test set WER: 23.26% (1st place), DER: 6.87%, SER: 66.16%. Ablation on dev set: fine-tuned CATT-Whisper baseline 30.43% WER → +regularized recipe 27.18% → +MC Dropout ensemble 26.02%.

Ablation

Adding the regularized training recipe (R-Drop, Focal Loss, high weight decay) reduces WER from 30.43% to 27.18% (3.25 pp gain), while MC Dropout ensembling further reduces to 26.02% (1.16 pp gain), confirming training regularization is the primary driver.

Limitations

['MC Dropout ensembling requires 200 forward passes (~50× slower than single pass), limiting practical deployment.', 'The individual contributions of R-Drop, Focal Loss, and weight decay are not disentangled.', 'Per-dialect performance is not analyzed, so dialect-specific weaknesses remain unknown.']

5) Why it matters

  1. Demonstrates that strong regularization can compensate for extreme data scarcity in multimodal settings, relevant to low-resource speech tasks.
  2. Shows that optimization strategy (regularization, hyperparameter tuning) can outperform architectural modifications when data is limited.
  3. Provides a practical recipe (R-Drop + Focal Loss + MC Dropout) that could transfer to other low-resource sequence labeling tasks.

6) Next steps

  • [ ] Disentangle individual contributions of R-Drop, Focal Loss, and weight decay via controlled ablation.
  • [ ] Analyze per-dialect performance to identify remaining challenges in dialectal Arabic diacritization.
  • [ ] Explore more efficient inference strategies (e.g., fewer MC passes, knowledge distillation) to reduce computational cost.
  • [ ] Apply the regularized fine-tuning recipe to other low-resource multimodal tasks (e.g., speech translation, emotion recognition).

深度笔记

Thaka在KSAA-2026任务2中的表现：面向阿拉伯语音节标注的正则化微调

主要结论 - KSAA-2026任务2的获胜系统仅使用2327个训练样本且无外部数据，实现了23.26%的WER。 - 训练正则化（R-Drop、Focal Loss、高权重衰减）相比标准微调带来3.25个百分点的WER提升。 - MC Dropout集成（4个检查点×200次随机前向传播）额外带来1.16个百分点的改进。 - 在低数据规模下，优化策略比架构修改更重要。 - 冻结的Whisper语音编码器与CATT文本编码器的多模态融合对阿拉伯语音节标注有效。

1) 阅读理由

本文表明，激进的训练正则化和推理时集成可以在极有限数据下实现最先进的阿拉伯语音节标注，优于架构修改。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

背景：阿拉伯语音节标注对TTS、机器翻译和语言学习至关重要，但由于歧义性（尤其是方言阿拉伯语）仍具挑战性。语音信号提供互补的消歧线索。KSAA-2026共享任务提供了一个低资源基准，仅2327个训练样本且不允许外部数据。

相关工作：CATT-Whisper（Ghannam等人，2025）结合CATT文本编码器和Whisper语音编码器的多模态架构，赢得了NADI 2025方言音节标注赛道。R-Drop（Liang等人，2021）通过惩罚两个dropout掩码前向传播之间的分歧预测进行一致性正则化。MC Dropout（Gal和Ghahramani，2016）在测试时保持dropout激活以实现类似集成的推理。AraBERT（Antoun等人，2020）和CATT（Alasmary等人，2024）是用于阿拉伯语音节标注的预训练编码器。Optuna（Akiba等人，2019）是用于调优训练配方的超参数优化框架。

差距：先前关于阿拉伯语音节标注的工作要么依赖大型数据集，要么专注于仅文本模型，难以处理歧义。KSAA-2026任务呈现了一个极低资源的多模态场景，其中CATT-Whisper的标准微调仅达到30.43%的WER。具体限制是如何在仅有2187个有效训练样本且无外部数据的情况下最大化性能。

方案：作者使用高度正则化的训练配方微调CATT-Whisper：R-Drop一致性损失、带标签平滑的Focal Loss、高权重衰减（0.098）以及Optuna优化的超参数。推理时，他们平均来自200次随机前向传播（4个检查点×50次MC Dropout前向传播）的softmax概率。这实现了23.26%的WER，在共享任务中排名第一。

3) 实验

主要结果：测试集WER为23.26%（第一名），DER为6.87%，SER为66.16%。在开发集上的消融实验：微调后的CATT-Whisper基线WER为30.43%，加上正则化配方后降至27.18%，再加上MC Dropout集成后进一步降至26.02%。

消融实验

添加正则化训练配方（R-Drop、Focal Loss、高权重衰减）使WER从30.43%降至27.18%（提升3.25个百分点），而MC Dropout集成进一步降至26.02%（提升1.16个百分点），证实训练正则化是主要驱动力。

局限性

['MC Dropout集成需要200次前向传播（比单次前向传播慢约50倍），限制了实际部署。', 'R-Drop、Focal Loss和权重衰减的各自贡献未被分离。', '未分析每种方言的性能，因此方言特定的弱点仍然未知。']

4) 重要性

  1. 证明了强正则化可以弥补多模态场景中的极端数据稀缺，对低资源语音任务具有参考价值。
  2. 表明在数据有限时，优化策略（正则化、超参数调优）可以胜过架构修改。
  3. 提供了一个实用的配方（R-Drop + Focal Loss + MC Dropout），可迁移到其他低资源序列标注任务。
