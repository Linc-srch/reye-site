Gemini 3 Deep Think: Advancing science, research and engineering

Gemini 3 Deep Think: Advancing science, research and engineering

2026-05-18 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google has released a major upgrade to Gemini 3 Deep Think, a specialized reasoning mode designed for science, research, and engineering. The update blends deep scientific knowledge with engineering utility, enabling practical applications like identifying logical flaws in peer-reviewed papers, optimizing crystal growth for semiconductor discovery, and accelerating physical component design. It achieves state-of-the-art results on benchmarks including Humanity's Last Exam (48.4%), ARC-AGI-2 (84.6%), and gold-medal performance on the International Math Olympiad 2025, as well as the Physics and Chemistry Olympiads. The model is available to Google AI Ultra subscribers and via the Gemini API through an early access program.

摘要

Google 发布了 Gemini 3 Deep Think 的重大升级，这是一种专为科学、研究和工程设计的 specialized reasoning mode。该更新融合了深厚的科学知识与工程实用性，能够实现实际应用，例如识别同行评审论文中的逻辑缺陷、优化晶体生长以发现半导体材料，以及加速物理组件设计。它在 Humanity's Last Exam（48.4%）、ARC-AGI-2（84.6%）等基准测试中取得了领先成绩，并在 International Math Olympiad 2025 以及 Physics 和 Chemistry Olympiads 中达到金牌水平。该模型现已面向 Google AI Ultra 订阅用户开放，并通过 Gemini API 的 early access program 提供。
