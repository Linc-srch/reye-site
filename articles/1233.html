Summary

The Anthropic Institute (TAI) announces its research agenda as of May 7, 2026, focusing on four areas: economic diffusion, threats and resilience, AI systems in the wild, and AI-driven R&D. Leveraging insider access at frontier lab Anthropic, TAI will study real-world AI impacts, including labor market changes, emerging security risks, and recursive self-improvement. It will share granular data, early warning signals, and tools to inform policymakers, researchers, and the public. The agenda is developed with Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust and aims to guide decisions on AI deployment for long-term human benefit, with a call for fellowship applications to tackle these questions.

摘要

The Anthropic Institute (TAI) 于2026年5月7日发布研究议程，聚焦经济扩散、威胁与韧性、野外的AI系统及AI驱动的研发。TAI利用前沿实验室Anthropic的内部视角，研究AI对就业、安全风险及递归自我改进的实际影响，并公开数据以协助政策制定。该议程由Anthropic的Long-Term Benefit Trust支持，旨在为AI的长期有益部署提供指导。
