We’re launching the Google DeepMind Accelerator program in Asia Pacific to tackle environmental risks

2026-05-22  ·  deepmind_blog  ·  Original →

Summary

Google DeepMind is launching an inaugural Accelerator program in Asia Pacific focused on 'AI for the Planet' to address environmental risks. The three-month program targets startups, research teams, and nonprofits, offering mentorship, tailored support, and integration of frontier AI and science AI models from Google AI experts. It aims to scale green technologies in nature, climate, agriculture, and energy sectors, starting with an in-person bootcamp in Singapore.

摘要

Google DeepMind在亚太地区启动首届Accelerator项目，聚焦“AI for the Planet”以应对环境风险。该项目为期三个月，面向初创企业、研究团队和非营利组织，提供专家指导、定制支持以及来自Google AI专家的前沿AI和科学AI模型集成，旨在加速自然、气候、农业和能源领域的绿色技术规模化，首站于新加坡举办线下训练营。
