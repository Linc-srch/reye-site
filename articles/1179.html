Gemini for Science: AI experiments and tools for a new era of discovery

Gemini for Science: AI experiments and tools for a new era of discovery

2026-05-20  ·  deepmind_blog  ·  Original →

Summary

Google introduces Gemini for Science, a suite of AI tools and experiments designed to accelerate scientific discovery. The collection includes three prototypes: Hypothesis Generation (built with Co-Scientist) for automated literature synthesis and idea generation, Computational Discovery (using AlphaEvolve and ERA) for parallel testing of code variations, and Literature Insights (powered by NotebookLM) for structured analysis of scientific papers. Additionally, Science Skills integrates over 30 life science databases for rapid genomic and bioinformatic analyses. These tools aim to serve as a force multiplier for researchers, enabling faster hypothesis testing and literature review. Google is collaborating with over 100 institutions and enterprise partners to validate and deploy these systems.

摘要

Google 推出 Gemini for Science，这是一套旨在加速科学发现的AI工具和实验。该系列包括三个原型：基于 Co-Scientist 的 Hypothesis Generation，用于自动文献综合和创意生成；使用 AlphaEvolve 和 ERA 的 Computational Discovery，用于并行测试代码变体；以及由 NotebookLM 驱动的 Literature Insights，用于结构化分析科学论文。此外，Science Skills 集成了超过30个生命科学数据库，用于快速基因组和生物信息学分析。这些工具旨在作为研究人员的倍增器，加速假设测试和文献回顾。Google 正在与超过100家机构和合作伙伴合作，验证和部署这些系统。
