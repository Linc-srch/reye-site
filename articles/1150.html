OpenAI launches DeployCo to help businesses build around intelligence

OpenAI launches DeployCo to help businesses build around intelligence

Summary

OpenAI announced the launch of DeployCo, a new standalone company focused on helping enterprises deploy AI systems into critical workflows. DeployCo will embed Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs) into client organizations to redesign infrastructure and processes around frontier AI. OpenAI also agreed to acquire Tomoro, an applied AI consulting firm, bringing approximately 150 experienced FDEs to DeployCo from day one. The initiative is backed by over $4 billion in initial investment from a consortium of global investment firms, consultancies, and system integrators, including TPG, Advent, and Bain Capital. DeployCo aims to accelerate real-world AI adoption and ensure durable operational impact.

摘要

OpenAI宣布成立DeployCo，这是一家专注于帮助企业将AI系统部署到关键工作流中的新公司。DeployCo将派遣Forward Deployed Engineers (FDEs)进入客户组织，围绕前沿AI重新设计基础设施和流程。OpenAI还同意收购应用AI咨询公司Tomoro，为DeployCo带来约150名经验丰富的FDEs。该计划获得了由TPG、Advent和Bain Capital等全球投资机构、咨询公司和系统集成商组成的财团超过40亿美元的初始投资。DeployCo旨在加速AI在实际场景中的采用，并确保持久的运营影响。
