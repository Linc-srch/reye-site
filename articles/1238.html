OpenAI named a Leader in enterprise coding agents by Gartner

OpenAI named a Leader in enterprise coding agents by Gartner

Summary

OpenAI has been named a Leader in the Gartner Magic Quadrant for Enterprise AI Coding Agents, reflecting the progress of its Codex product. Codex is used by over 4 million people weekly and by companies such as Cisco, Datadog, Dell, and NVIDIA. The report highlights Codex's strengths in agentic software development, enterprise governance, sandboxing, and flexible deployment. OpenAI has since improved Codex with GPT-5.5, stronger tool use, faster performance, and deeper enterprise workflow support. Cisco used Codex to build most of its AI Defense platform, shortening delivery from quarters to weeks. The recognition underscores the shift from autocomplete to delegating complex tasks to AI agents.

摘要

OpenAI被Gartner评为企业AI编码代理领域的领导者，反映了其Codex产品的进展。Codex每周有超过400万用户，包括Cisco、Datadog、Dell和NVIDIA等企业。Gartner报告认可Codex在代理软件开发、企业治理、沙箱和灵活部署方面的优势。OpenAI随后通过GPT-5.5增强了Codex的工具使用、性能和流程支持。Cisco使用Codex构建了其AI Defense平台的大部分，将交付时间从几个季度缩短到几周。
