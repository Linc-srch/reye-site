Announcing our partnership with the Republic of Korea

Announcing our partnership with the Republic of Korea

content-management digital-publishing rlhf
Summary

Google DeepMind announces a partnership with the Republic of Korea's Ministry of Science and ICT (MSIT) to accelerate scientific discovery using frontier AI. The collaboration includes establishing an AI Campus in Seoul, leveraging models like AlphaEvolve, AlphaGenome, AlphaFold, AI co-scientist, and WeatherNext for life sciences, energy, and climate. It also focuses on cultivating local AI talent through internships and scholarships, and partnering with the Korean AI Safety Institute (AISI) on safety research. Building on the AlphaGo legacy, this initiative aims to support Korea's AI strategy and national grand challenges.

摘要

Google DeepMind与韩国科学技术信息通信部（MSIT）宣布合作，利用前沿AI加速科学发现。合作包括在首尔建立AI Campus，应用AlphaEvolve、AlphaGenome、AlphaFold、AI co-scientist和WeatherNext等模型于生命科学、能源和气候领域。同时通过实习和奖学金培养本地AI人才，并与韩国AI Safety Institute（AISI）合作开展安全研究。此举旨在延续AlphaGo遗产，支持韩国AI战略和国家重大挑战。
