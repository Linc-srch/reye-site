Newsroom

2026-05-19 · anthropic_news

Summary

Newsroom is a system developed by the New York Times for managing and publishing news content. It integrates various editorial tools, workflows, and data to support the production of digital and print journalism. The platform enables journalists to write, edit, and collaborate on stories, while also managing multimedia assets and metadata. Newsroom aims to streamline the news production process and improve efficiency in the newsroom environment.

摘要

Newsroom是纽约时报开发的新闻内容管理与发布系统，集成了编辑工具、工作流和数据，支持数字与印刷新闻生产。平台让记者可以写作、编辑和协作，同时管理多媒体资产和元数据，旨在简化新闻生产流程并提高效率。
