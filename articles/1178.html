Gemini 3.5: frontier intelligence with action

Gemini 3.5: frontier intelligence with action

Summary

Google DeepMind introduces Gemini 3.5, a new family of frontier models designed for agentic workflows and coding. The initial release, 3.5 Flash, delivers flagship-level intelligence with exceptional speed, outperforming previous models on benchmarks like Terminal-Bench 2.1 and CharXiv Reasoning. It excels at long-horizon tasks, enabling developers and enterprises to automate complex workflows at lower cost. Powered by the Antigravity harness, it supports collaborative subagents for scalable problem-solving. The model is integrated into the Gemini app, Google Search, and enterprise platforms, with safety measures aligned with the Frontier Safety Framework. A 3.5 Pro version is forthcoming.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布了 Gemini 3.5 系列模型，专注于 agentic 工作流和编码。首发版本 3.5 Flash 在 Terminal-Bench 2.1 和 CharXiv Reasoning 等基准测试中表现领先，兼具旗舰级智能与极速推理。它擅长处理长周期任务，通过 Antigravity 框架支持多子智能体协作，显著降低自动化复杂流程的成本。该模型已集成至 Gemini 应用、Google Search 及企业平台，并遵循 Frontier Safety Framework 强化安全防护。3.5 Pro 版本预计下月推出。
