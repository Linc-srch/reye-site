Summary

Anthropic has launched the Anthropic Economic Index Survey, a monthly qualitative study conducted via Anthropic Interviewer. The survey targets a random sample of Claude users to capture first-hand accounts of how AI is affecting work, productivity, hiring, and role expectations. By collecting these insights monthly, Anthropic aims to detect changes earlier than traditional labor market indicators. The data will be combined with privacy-preserving Claude usage data to inform economic research and future model improvements. The survey begins immediately, with a new set of users invited each month.

摘要

Anthropic推出了Anthropic Economic Index Survey，这是一项通过Anthropic Interviewer进行的月度定性调查。调查随机邀请Claude用户，收集AI对工作、生产力、招聘和角色期望影响的真实反馈。通过每月采集数据，目的是比传统劳动力指标更早发现变化。这些数据将与隐私保护的Claude使用数据结合，用于经济研究和模型改进。调查已启动，每月更换受邀用户。
