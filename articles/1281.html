Windows Zero-Day Barrage Continues After Patch Tuesday

Windows Zero-Day Barrage Continues After Patch Tuesday

Summary

This article reports on a continued wave of Windows zero-day vulnerabilities being exploited in the wild shortly after Microsoft's Patch Tuesday updates. It highlights the persistent threat landscape where attackers rapidly weaponize newly disclosed flaws before patches can be widely deployed. The piece underscores the challenge for security teams in prioritizing and applying fixes amidst a barrage of critical vulnerabilities affecting core Windows components.

摘要

本文报道了微软Patch Tuesday更新后，Windows零日漏洞持续被野外利用的情况。文章强调攻击者迅速武器化新披露漏洞的威胁态势，以及安全团队在大量关键Windows组件漏洞中优先修复的挑战。
