A note on convergence of Wasserstein policy optimization

关于Wasserstein策略优化收敛性的注记

David Šiška, Yufei Zhang

Summary

This paper provides the first theoretical guarantee of linear convergence for Wasserstein Policy Optimization in continuous action spaces, bridging a gap between empirical success and theoretical understanding.

摘要

本文首次证明了Wasserstein策略优化（WPO）在连续动作空间的熵正则化MDP中具有线性收敛到全局最优的理论保证。通过平均场分析和对数Sobolev不等式，建立了单调能量耗散和指数收敛性，弥合了WPO实证成功与理论理解之间的鸿沟。

Deep Note

Deep Note — wpo-convergence

Key Takeaways - Wasserstein Policy Optimization (WPO) converges linearly to the global optimum in entropy-regularized MDPs with continuous action spaces. - The proof leverages mean-field analysis and log-Sobolev inequalities to establish monotonic energy dissipation and exponential convergence. - The analysis assumes a sufficiently regular solution to the gradient flow equation and a local log-Sobolev inequality along the flow. - The result holds for discrete finite state spaces and continuous action spaces under a discounted infinite-horizon setting.

2) CRGP

C — Context

Policy optimization in reinforcement learning has seen significant empirical success, with methods like PPO and SAC dominating continuous control tasks. Wasserstein Policy Optimization (WPO) was recently proposed as an actor-critic algorithm that approximates Wasserstein gradient flows over policy spaces, showing strong empirical performance but lacking theoretical convergence guarantees, especially in continuous state and action spaces.

R — Related work

  • Mean-field Langevin dynamics and its convergence analysis using log-Sobolev inequalities (e.g., Chizat & Bach, 2018; Mei et al., 2018; Hu et al., 2021).
  • Entropy-regularized MDPs and their optimal policies expressed as Gibbs distributions (e.g., Haarnoja et al., 2017; Geist et al., 2019).
  • Wasserstein gradient flows in machine learning (e.g., Arbel et al., 2019; Salim et al., 2020).
  • Convergence of policy gradient methods in continuous spaces (e.g., Agarwal et al., 2020; Bhandari & Russo, 2021).

G — Gap

While WPO has demonstrated empirical success, its theoretical convergence properties in environments with continuous state and action spaces remained unproven. Existing convergence analyses for policy optimization often rely on convexity or smoothness assumptions that do not directly apply to the Wasserstein gradient flow formulation.

P — Proposal

The paper analyzes WPO within the framework of entropy-regularized MDPs, modeling policy evolution as a Wasserstein gradient flow. By assuming the existence of a sufficiently regular solution to the gradient flow equation, they prove monotonic energy dissipation and establish a local log-Sobolev inequality, which together imply linear convergence of the value function to the global optimum.

主要结论 - Wasserstein策略优化（WPO）在连续动作空间的熵正则化MDP中线性收敛到全局最优。 - 证明利用平均场分析和对数Sobolev不等式，建立了单调能量耗散和指数收敛。 - 分析假设梯度流方程存在足够正则的解，且沿流满足局部对数Sobolev不等式。 - 结果适用于离散有限状态空间和连续动作空间，在折扣无限时域设定下成立。

2) CRGP（背景 / 相关工作 / 差距 / 方案）

上下文：强化学习中的策略优化在连续控制任务中取得了显著实证成功，如PPO和SAC。Wasserstein策略优化（WPO）最近被提出作为一种演员-评论家算法，近似策略空间上的Wasserstein梯度流，表现出强实证性能但缺乏理论收敛保证，尤其在连续状态和动作空间中。相关工作：平均场Langevin动力学及其使用对数Sobolev不等式的收敛分析（如Chizat & Bach, 2018; Mei et al., 2018; Hu et al., 2021）；熵正则化MDP及其最优策略的Gibbs分布表示（如Haarnoja et al., 2017; Geist et al., 2019）；机器学习中的Wasserstein梯度流（如Arbel et al., 2019; Salim et al., 2020）；连续空间中策略梯度方法的收敛性（如Agarwal et al., 2020; Bhandari & Russo, 2021）。差距：尽管WPO已展示实证成功，其在连续状态和动作空间环境中的理论收敛性质仍未得到证明。现有的策略优化收敛分析通常依赖于凸性或光滑性假设，这些假设不直接适用于Wasserstein梯度流公式。提案：本文在熵正则化MDP框架内分析WPO，将策略演化建模为Wasserstein梯度流。通过假设梯度流方程存在足够正则的解，证明了单调能量耗散并建立了局部对数Sobolev不等式，从而推导出值函数线性收敛到全局最优。
