How business operations teams use Codex

How business operations teams use Codex

2026-05-19 · openai_rss

Summary

This article from OpenAI Academy explains how business operations teams can leverage Codex to streamline the creation of decision-ready artifacts. It details four primary use cases: initiative off-track briefs, strategic initiative health updates, leadership decision packets, and board or company progress updates. For each use case, the article specifies the inputs the team provides (e.g., project trackers, KPI dashboards, meeting notes) and the structured outputs Codex generates (e.g., executive briefs, decision packets). The emphasis is on Codex handling the initial draft, allowing the team to focus on judgment, recommendations, and stakeholder review, thereby accelerating the decision-making process.

摘要

本文来自OpenAI Academy，阐述了业务运营团队如何利用Codex将分散的项目背景、指标、追踪器和利益相关者输入转化为决策就绪的简报、更新、资料包和权衡模型。文章详细介绍了四个主要用例：计划偏离轨道简报、战略计划健康更新、领导层决策包以及董事会或公司进度更新。对于每个用例，文章明确了团队提供的输入（如项目追踪器、KPI仪表盘、会议记录）以及Codex生成的结构化输出（如高管简报、决策包）。重点在于Codex处理初稿，让团队专注于判断、建议和利益相关者审查，从而加速决策过程。
