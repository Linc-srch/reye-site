Helping ChatGPT better recognize context in sensitive conversations

Helping ChatGPT better recognize context in sensitive conversations

content-management digital-publishing rlhf AI Safety & Alignment Governance & Policy
Summary

OpenAI introduces safety updates for ChatGPT to better recognize evolving risk in sensitive conversations, particularly suicide, self-harm, and harm-to-others scenarios. The system uses context-aware training and safety summaries—short factual notes about prior safety-relevant context—to distinguish benign interactions from high-risk ones. These summaries are created by a safety reasoning model, stored temporarily, and used only for serious concerns. Internal evaluations show significant improvements: safe-response performance improved by 50% in suicide/self-harm cases and 16% in harm-to-others within single conversations, and by 52% and 39% respectively across multiple conversations on GPT-5.5 Instant. The work was guided by mental health experts and aims to de-escalate, refuse harmful details, or redirect to safer alternatives without overreacting in ordinary chats.

摘要

OpenAI 为 ChatGPT 推出安全更新，使其能更好地识别敏感对话中随时间演变的潜在风险，特别是自杀、自伤和伤害他人场景。系统通过上下文感知训练和 safety summaries（由安全推理模型生成的简短事实性笔记，临时存储且仅用于严重关切）来区分良性互动与高风险情况。内部评估显示显著改进：在单次对话中，自杀/自伤场景的安全响应性能提升 50%，伤害他人场景提升 16%；在 GPT-5.5 Instant 上跨对话场景分别提升 52% 和 39%。该工作由心理健康专家指导，旨在通过缓和、拒绝有害细节或引导至更安全替代方案来恰当回应，同时避免在日常对话中过度反应。
