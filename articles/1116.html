Partnering with industry leaders to accelerate AI transformation

Partnering with industry leaders to accelerate AI transformation

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Google DeepMind announces partnerships with Accenture, Bain & Company, BCG, Deloitte, and McKinsey to accelerate enterprise AI adoption. The collaboration aims to bridge the AI adoption gap, as only 25% of organizations have scaled AI into production. The initiative focuses on three pillars: enabling industry-specific AI capabilities, providing early access to frontier models like Gemini, and connecting AI leadership with customer executives. The goal is to drive responsible AI transformation across sectors such as finance, manufacturing, retail, and media, contributing to global economic growth.

摘要

Google DeepMind宣布与Accenture、Bain & Company、BCG、Deloitte和McKinsey合作，加速企业AI应用。该合作旨在弥合AI采用差距，目前仅25%的组织将AI规模化投入生产。计划聚焦三大支柱：开发行业特定AI能力、提供Gemini等前沿模型的早期访问、以及连接AI领导层与客户高管。目标是在金融、制造、零售和媒体等领域推动负责任的AI转型，促进全球经济增长。
