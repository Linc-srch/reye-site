From games to biology and beyond: 10 years of AlphaGo’s impact

From games to biology and beyond: 10 years of AlphaGo’s impact

2026-05-19 · deepmind_blog

Summary

Ten years ago, AlphaGo defeated a world champion at Go, marking the start of the modern AI era. Its creative 'Move 37' demonstrated AI's potential beyond human intuition. Using deep neural networks, reinforcement learning, and search, AlphaGo tackled the game's vast complexity. This breakthrough led to AlphaFold, which solved protein folding and won a Nobel Prize, and inspired systems like AlphaProof for mathematical reasoning, AlphaEvolve for algorithm discovery, and AI co-scientists. DeepMind now integrates these techniques into Gemini, aiming toward artificial general intelligence (AGI) to solve major scientific and societal challenges.

摘要

十年前，AlphaGo击败围棋世界冠军，开启了现代AI时代。其创造性的‘Move 37’展示了AI超越人类直觉的潜力。通过深度神经网络、reinforcement learning和搜索技术，AlphaGo攻克了围棋的巨大复杂性。这一突破催生了AlphaFold，解决了蛋白质折叠问题并获诺贝尔奖，还启发了AlphaProof（数学推理）、AlphaEvolve（算法发现）和AI co-scientist等系统。DeepMind正将这些技术整合到Gemini中，迈向artificial general intelligence (AGI)，以解决重大科学和社会挑战。
