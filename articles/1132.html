OpenAI and Malta partner to bring ChatGPT Plus to all citizens

2026-05-19 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI and the Government of Malta have announced a pioneering partnership to provide ChatGPT Plus to all Maltese citizens. The initiative includes an AI literacy course developed by the University of Malta, designed to teach responsible AI use. Upon completion, citizens receive free access to ChatGPT Plus for one year. This program, managed by the Malta Digital Innovation Authority, aims to empower citizens with practical AI skills and position Malta as a global leader in national AI adoption. The partnership reflects OpenAI's vision of making intelligence a global utility and is part of the OpenAI for Countries initiative, tailored to local priorities.

摘要

OpenAI与马耳他政府宣布开创性合作，为所有马耳他公民提供ChatGPT Plus。项目包括由马耳他大学开发的AI素养课程，教授负责任地使用AI。完成课程后，公民可免费使用ChatGPT Plus一年。该计划由马耳他数字创新管理局管理，旨在赋能公民掌握实用AI技能，使马耳他成为国家AI应用的全球领导者。此次合作体现了OpenAI将智能作为全球公用事业的愿景，是OpenAI for Countries计划的一部分，根据当地需求定制。
