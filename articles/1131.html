OpenAI and Dell partner to bring Codex to hybrid and on-premise enterprise environments

OpenAI and Dell partner to bring Codex to hybrid and on-premise enterprise environments

content-management editorial-workflow digital-publishing
Summary

OpenAI and Dell Technologies have partnered to deploy Codex in hybrid and on-premises enterprise environments. Codex, used by over 4 million developers weekly, is expanding beyond coding into tasks like report preparation and lead qualification. The collaboration integrates Codex with Dell's AI Data Platform and AI Factory, enabling secure, governed access to enterprise data and systems. This allows businesses to deploy AI agents closer to their existing infrastructure, supporting software development and knowledge work with enhanced context and control, providing a practical path to production-scale AI adoption.

摘要

OpenAI与Dell Technologies合作，将Codex部署到混合云和本地企业环境中。Codex每周有超过400万开发者使用，其应用已从代码开发扩展到报告准备和线索筛选等任务。此次合作将Codex与Dell AI Data Platform及Dell AI Factory集成，使企业能在安全、受控的环境下访问内部数据与系统。这有助于企业在现有基础设施上部署AI代理，提升软件开发与知识工作的上下文感知能力，为生产级AI应用提供可行路径。
