What It'll Take to Make AI BOMs Usable in a Modern Security Program

What It'll Take to Make AI BOMs Usable in a Modern Security Program

Summary

The article discusses the challenges and strategies for making AI Bills of Materials (AI BOMs) operationally useful in cybersecurity programs. It notes that while standards and tools are emerging, most organizations lack visibility into their AI systems and struggle to generate or consume AI BOMs effectively. Key steps include scoping AI assets, integrating AI BOMs into incident response and governance workflows, and addressing data provenance to ensure trust. The piece emphasizes that AI BOMs must be machine-readable and actionable, not just filed away, and that CISOs should leverage existing software security practices while tackling unique AI-specific risks like poisoned training data.

摘要

文章探讨了如何使AI BOM在网络安全项目中具备可操作性。尽管标准和工具正在成熟，但多数组织对AI系统缺乏可见性，难以生成或使用AI BOM。关键步骤包括：界定AI资产范围、将AI BOM集成到事件响应和治理工作流中，以及解决数据来源问题以确保信任。文章强调AI BOM必须是机器可读且可操作的，而非仅归档。CISO应借鉴现有软件安全实践，同时应对AI特有的风险，如训练数据投毒。
