Our response to the TanStack npm supply chain attack

Our response to the TanStack npm supply chain attack

2026-05-18 · openai_rss

Summary

OpenAI disclosed a security incident involving the TanStack npm library, part of the broader Mini Shai-Hulud supply chain attack. Two employee devices were impacted, leading to unauthorized access and credential exfiltration from a limited set of internal source code repositories. No evidence of compromise to user data, production systems, or intellectual property was found. As a precaution, OpenAI is rotating code-signing certificates, requiring macOS users to update applications by June 12, 2026. The company has implemented additional security controls and is coordinating with platform providers to prevent unauthorized certificate use.

摘要

OpenAI披露了一起涉及TanStack npm库的安全事件，该事件属于更广泛的Mini Shai-Hulud供应链攻击。两台员工设备受到影响，导致对有限内部源代码仓库的未授权访问和凭证窃取。未发现用户数据、生产系统或知识产权受损的证据。作为预防措施，OpenAI正在轮换代码签名证书，要求macOS用户在2026年6月12日前更新应用程序。公司已实施额外的安全控制，并与平台提供商协调以防止证书被滥用。
