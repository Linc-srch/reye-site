Introducing Gemini Omni

Introducing Gemini Omni

Summary

Google DeepMind introduces Gemini Omni, a natively multimodal model that can generate and edit videos from any combination of inputs—text, images, audio, and video. The first model, Gemini Omni Flash, is rolling out in the Gemini app, Google Flow, and YouTube Shorts. It supports natural language video editing with consistent character and physics, real-world knowledge grounding, and multi-turn refinement. Features include transforming scenes, reimagining actions, and creating videos from references. Outputs include SynthID watermarks for transparency. Future updates will add image and audio output modalities.

摘要

Google DeepMind 发布 Gemini Omni，这是一个原生多模态模型，能从文本、图像、音频和视频等任意输入生成并编辑视频。首个模型 Gemini Omni Flash 已在 Gemini 应用、Google Flow 和 YouTube Shorts 中推出。它支持通过自然语言进行视频编辑，保持角色和物理一致性，并基于 Gemini 的世界知识进行推理。功能包括场景变换、动作重想象和基于参考的视频生成。所有输出均包含 SynthID 水印以确保透明度。未来将支持图像和音频输出。
