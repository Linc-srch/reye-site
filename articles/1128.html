A new way to express yourself: Gemini can now create music

A new way to express yourself: Gemini can now create music

Summary

Google's Gemini app now integrates Lyria 3, an advanced music generation model from DeepMind, allowing users to create 30-second tracks from text prompts or uploaded images. The feature offers creative control over style, vocals, and tempo, with automatic lyric generation. All outputs are watermarked with SynthID for AI content identification. Available in beta for users 18+ in multiple languages, with higher limits for subscribers. The tool aims to facilitate creative expression rather than produce masterpieces, and is also rolling out to YouTube's Dream Track for Shorts creators.

摘要

Google的Gemini应用集成了DeepMind的最新音乐生成模型Lyria 3，用户可通过文本描述或上传图片生成30秒的音乐轨道。该功能支持对风格、人声和节奏的创意控制，并自动生成歌词。所有输出均嵌入SynthID水印以标识AI内容。Beta版面向18岁以上用户开放，支持多种语言，订阅用户享有更高使用限制。该工具旨在促进创意表达，而非创作音乐杰作，同时也在YouTube的Dream Track中向Shorts创作者推出。
