What Will Make AI BOMs Real?

What Will Make AI BOMs Real?

content-management supply-chain-attack zero-trust
Summary

The article examines the growing momentum behind AI Bill of Materials (BOMs), driven by standards bodies, open-source projects, commercial vendors, and regulatory pressures. Key developments include OWASP's CycloneDX and Linux Foundation's SPDX AI extensions, the OpenSSF model-signing specification, and CISA's foundational guidance. Commercial tools from Manifest Cyber, Cycode, JFrog, and Apiiro are emerging. Regulatory drivers like the EU AI Act and US NDAA requirements are pushing adoption. Cyber insurers are also conditioning coverage on AI governance documentation. Despite progress, widespread adoption remains aspirational as the industry races to finalize standards and tooling before regulatory deadlines.

摘要

本文探讨了推动AI BOM（人工智能物料清单）成为现实的多股力量。OWASP的CycloneDX和Linux基金会的SPDX已发布AI专用扩展，OpenSSF制定了模型签名规范，CISA发布了基础指南。商业方面，Manifest Cyber、Cycode、JFrog等厂商推出了AI BOM生成与管理工具。监管压力是主要驱动力，EU AI Act和US NDAA要求供应商在SBOM中纳入AI组件。网络保险公司也开始将AI治理文档作为承保条件。尽管进展显著，但AI BOM的广泛采用仍处于理想阶段，行业正争分夺秒地在监管截止日期前完善标准与工具。
