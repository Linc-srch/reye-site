Summary

This article discusses strategies and frameworks for protecting individuals from harmful manipulation, particularly in digital environments where AI and algorithms can influence behavior. It emphasizes the need for transparency, user agency, and regulatory measures to prevent exploitation through deceptive design, misinformation, and psychological targeting. The piece highlights the ethical responsibilities of technology developers and policymakers in safeguarding vulnerable populations.

摘要

本文探讨了保护个人免受有害操纵的策略与框架，尤其是在AI和算法可能影响行为的数字环境中。强调透明度、用户自主权以及监管措施的必要性，以防止通过欺骗性设计、错误信息和心理定位进行的剥削。文章突出了技术开发者和政策制定者在保护弱势群体方面的伦理责任。
