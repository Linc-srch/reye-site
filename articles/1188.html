Opening new paths in aging research

Opening new paths in aging research

2026-05-20  ·  deepmind_blog  ·  Original →

Summary

Google DeepMind's Co-Scientist, developed with Calico Life Sciences, aids aging research by synthesizing scattered biological findings into testable hypotheses. It helped generate a novel hypothesis on how metabolism regulates the integrated stress response (ISR), a cellular mechanism linked to aging and disease. Researchers used Co-Scientist to refine experimental designs and incorporate new data, leading to significant findings on ISR's role in health. The tool's scientific discernment impressed experts, cutting through noisy literature to identify promising research directions.

摘要

Google DeepMind的Co-Scientist与Calico Life Sciences合作，通过整合零散的生物学发现生成可验证的假设，助力衰老研究。它帮助提出了关于代谢如何调控integrated stress response (ISR)的新颖假说，ISR是一种与衰老和疾病相关的细胞机制。研究人员利用Co-Scientist优化实验设计并整合新数据，取得了关于ISR在健康中作用的重要发现。该工具的科学辨别力给专家留下深刻印象，能过滤嘈杂文献，识别有前景的研究方向。
