Decoupled DiLoCo: A new frontier for resilient, distributed AI training

deepmind_blog · 2026-05-19
Decoupled DiLoCo, introduced by Google DeepMind, is a distributed training architecture that partitions large language model training into decoupled 'islands' of compute with asynchronous data flow. This design isolates hardware failures, allowing other islands to continue training uninterrupted, and supports self-healing reintegration of failed units. It dramatically reduces bandwidth requirements compared to conventional synchronization methods, enabling training across distant data centers with internet-scale connectivity. Experiments with Gemma 4 models showed that Decoupled DiLoCo maintains high goodput under hardware failures and achieves equivalent ML performance to traditional approaches, while also allowing mixing of different hardware generations like TPU v6e and TPU v5p.
Google DeepMind 提出的 Decoupled DiLoCo 是一种分布式训练架构，将大语言模型训练划分为解耦的“计算岛”，通过异步数据流连接。这种设计隔离了硬件故障，使其他岛能继续高效训练，并支持故障单元的自愈重连。相比传统同步方法，它大幅降低了带宽需求，支持通过互联网级连接在远程数据中心间训练。使用 Gemma 4 模型的实验表明，Decoupled DiLoCo 在硬件故障下保持高有效训练产出，并达到与传统方法相同的 ML 性能，同时允许混合不同代硬件如 TPU v6e 和 TPU v5p。
