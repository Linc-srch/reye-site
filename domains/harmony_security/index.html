Research Direction

研究方向

Domain: Artificial Intelligence Security, Mobile & On-Device Security, Privacy Protection Core Research Objective: Monitoring, analyzing, and deriving strategic insights from industry intelligence, emerging security technologies, and next-generation products at the intersection of AI (Large Language Models, Agents) and cyber security. Taxonomy of Research Areas: 1. Industry Intelligence & AI Security Landscape: Tracking and analyzing global technology trends, security announcements, and strategic shifts from industry-leading organizations (such as Google, OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, Anthropic, etc.). 2. Advanced AI/Agent Security Technologies & Defenses: Evaluating cutting-edge paradigms in AI-driven protection and defensive mechanisms, focusing on how leading firms secure their LLM and autonomous agent ecosystems. 3. Threat Vectors, Vulnerabilities & Data Privacy: Investigating novel threat vectors, exploitation techniques, model vulnerabilities, and risk factors including privacy leakage and enterprise data breaches within AI deployments. 4. On-Device Agent & Endpoint Security: Exploring the specialized security architectures, local privacy boundaries, and threat mitigation frameworks required for deploying autonomous agents natively on end-user devices (mobile/IoT). 领域： 人工智能安全、移动与端侧安全、隐私保护 核心研究目标： 持续追踪、分析并洞察人工智能（大语言模型、智能体）与网络安全交叉领域的行业情报、前沿安全技术以及次世代产品动态。 研究领域分类： 1. 行业情报与 AI 安全态势： 追踪和分析全球头部科技企业（如 Google、OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, Anthropic 等）在安全领域的最新行业动态、技术发布与战略演进。 2. 先进 AI/Agent 安全技术与防御： 评估基于 AI 的前沿防护与防御机制，重点关注行业领先公司如何为其大语言模型及自主智能体生态系统构建全方位的安全防护网。 3. 威胁向量、漏洞与数据隐私： 研究 AI 部署中出现的新型威胁向量、漏洞利用技术、模型自身缺陷，以及隐私泄露和企业数据泄露等高危风险。 端侧 Agent 与终端安全： 探索在终端用户设备（移动端/物联网）上原生部署自主智能体所需的专属安全架构、本地隐私控制边界以及威胁缓解框架。

面向移动GUI导航的视觉语言智能体：规模扩展、基准测试与推理

This paper systematically studies data scaling, benchmarking, and reasoning for VLM-based mobile GUI agents, introducing a large-scale dataset (HyperTrack) and a unified evaluation toolkit (GUIEvalKit) to show that reinforcement-based finetuning outperforms supervised finetuning, especially in out-of-domain settings.

本文系统研究了基于视觉语言模型（VLM）的移动GUI智能体在数据规模扩展、基准测试和推理方面的关键问题。作者构建了包含超过16,000个真实世界任务、覆盖674个中文移动应用的大规模数据集HyperTrack，并推出了统一离线评测工具包GUIEvalKit。实验表明，基于强化学习的微调（DAPO风格RL）在域内和域外任务上均显著优于监督微调，且性能随训练数据量的对数近似线性增长。
