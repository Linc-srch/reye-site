Research Direction

研究方向

Domain: Cybersecurity, Artificial Intelligence, Security Operations Center (SOC) Core Research Objective: Leveraging Artificial Intelligence to empower modern security operations, system security, and incident response, while simultaneously establishing robust security governance, protection, and risk control frameworks for AI models and multi-agent systems. Taxonomy of Research Areas: 1. AI for Security (AI4Sec) & Intelligent Operations: Utilizing machine learning, Graph Neural Networks (GNNs), Large Language Models (LLMs), and autonomous agents to revolutionize detection and automated response capabilities. Key technical mechanisms include: • Anomaly Detection & Threat Analysis: Implementing intelligent models for proactive anomaly spotting and deep threat assessment. • Attack Correlation & Automated Operations: Reconstructing complex attack lifecycles and automating SOC workflow orchestrations. 2. Security for AI (Sec4AI) & AI Agent Governance: Researching safety guardrails, governance frameworks, and defense vectors for AI models and autonomous agent ecosystems. Focus areas include: • Adversarial Defense & Vulnerability Mitigation: Defending against prompt injection, model abuse, and training/inference data leaks. • Agent Permission Governance: Managing identity, access controls, and operational boundaries for multi-agent systems to mitigate runtime risks. 3. Threat Intelligence & Behavioral Profiling: Constructing cooperative cyber threat intelligence systems and mapping out threat landscapes. Key implementations include: • Relational Intelligence Analysis: Interconnecting CVEs, Nuclei templates, Indicators of Compromise (IOC), and threat feeds to map complex intelligence graphs. • Attack Behavior Profiling: Fingerprinting malicious actors and conducting end-to-end attack chain analysis for faster incident response. 4. System & Cloud-Native Security: Securing infrastructure baselines and distributed modern workloads. Focus areas include: • Cloud-Native Infrastructure: Hardening container runtimes, Kubernetes clusters, and cloud-native network layers against unauthorized lateral movement. 领域： 网络安全、人工智能、安全运营中心 (SOC) 核心研究目标： 利用人工智能赋能现代安全运营、系统安全与应急响应，同时针对人工智能模型及多智能体系统构建稳健的安全治理、防护与风险控制框架。 研究领域分类： 1. 人工智能赋能安全 (AI for Security / AI4Sec) 与智能运营： 利用机器学习、图神经网络 (GNN)、大语言模型 (LLM) 与自主智能体 (Agent) 颠覆传统检测与自动化响应能力。关键技术机制包括： • 异常检测与威胁分析： 部署智能模型以实现主动的异常发现和深度的威胁评估。 • 攻击关联与自动运营： 重构复杂的攻击生命周期，并实现安全运营中心 (SOC) 工作流的自动化编排。 2. 人工智能自身安全 (Security for AI / Sec4AI) 与智能体安全治理： 研究针对 AI 模型和自主智能体生态的安全红线、治理框架和防御向量。重点领域包括： • 对抗性防御与漏洞缓解： 抵御提示词注入 (Prompt Injection)、模型滥用以及训练/推理阶段的数据安全风险。 • 智能体权限治理： 管理多智能体系统的身份、访问控制和行为边界，以维护其运行时的风险控制。 3. 威胁情报与行为画像： 建设协同的网络威胁情报体系并绘制威胁全景图。关键实现包括： • 情报关系分析： 将 CVE、Nuclei 漏洞扫描模板、网络威胁指示物 (IOC) 以及情报源进行关联，构建复杂的威胁情报图谱。 • 攻击行为画像： 对恶意攻击者进行特征指纹提取，并进行端到端的攻击链分析，以加速应急响应。 4. 系统与云原生安全： 保障基础设施底层和分布式现代工作负载的安全。重点领域包括： • 云原生基础设施： 对容器运行时、Kubernetes 集群以及云原生网络层进行安全加固，防止未经授权的横向移动。
