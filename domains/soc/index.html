Research Direction

研究方向

Domain: Artificial Intelligence Security, Operating System (OS) Security, Mobile & Endpoint Security, Data Security & Privacy Protection Core Research Objective: Monitoring, analyzing, and deriving strategic insights from industry intelligence to benchmark HarmonyOS endpoint security against major ecosystems (Apple iOS, Google Android, Microsoft Windows). The research spans foundational OS security, data privacy frameworks, and emerging technologies at the intersection of AI (LLMs, Agents) and cybersecurity within edge-cloud environments. Taxonomy of Research Areas: 1. OS Security & Ecosystem Benchmarking: Tracking and analyzing security architectures, system-level implementations, and strategic shifts from industry-leading OS providers (Apple, Google, Microsoft) to establish comprehensive competitive intelligence and baseline standards for HarmonyOS. 2. Data Security & Privacy Protection: Investigating robust data protection mechanisms, local privacy boundaries, data leakage prevention (DLP), and compliance across mobile and desktop endpoint environments. 3. Industry Intelligence & AI Security Landscape: Tracking and analyzing global technology trends, security announcements, and strategic shifts from industry-leading organizations (such as Google, OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, Anthropic, etc.). 4. Advanced AI/Agent Security Technologies & Defenses: Evaluating how leading ecosystems secure their large language models and autonomous agent workflows against adversarial attacks, prompt injections, and system-level exploitation. 5. Threat Vectors, Vulnerabilities & Data Privacy: Investigating novel threat vectors, exploitation techniques, model vulnerabilities, and risk factors including privacy leakage and enterprise data breaches within AI deployments. 6. On-Device Agent & Endpoint Security: Exploring specialized security architectures for deploying autonomous AI agents natively on end-user devices (Mobile/IoT), ensuring strict access control, edge-cloud secure communication, and robust threat mitigation frameworks. 领域： 人工智能安全、操作系统 (OS) 安全、移动与端侧安全、数据安全与隐私保护 核心研究目标： 持续追踪、分析行业情报并获取战略洞察，将鸿蒙端侧安全与主流操作系统生态（苹果 iOS、谷歌 Android、微软 Windows）进行全面对标。研究范围涵盖底层操作系统安全、数据隐私框架以及人工智能（大语言模型、智能体）与网络安全交叉领域在端云协同环境下的前沿技术。 研究领域分类： 1. OS 安全与生态对标 (OS Security & Ecosystem Benchmarking)： 追踪和分析行业领先操作系统提供商（苹果、谷歌、微软）的安全架构、系统级实现与战略演进，为鸿蒙系统建立全面的竞争情报体系与基线标准。 2. 数据安全与隐私保护 (Data Security & Privacy Protection)： 研究移动与桌面终端环境下的高强度数据保护机制、本地隐私控制边界、防数据泄露 (DLP) 技术以及安全合规性。 3. 行业情报与 AI 安全态势： 追踪和分析全球头部科技企业（如 Google、OpenAI, Meta, Microsoft, Anthropic 等）在安全领域的最新行业动态、技术发布与战略演进。 4. 先进 AI/Agent 安全技术与防御 (Advanced AI/Agent Security Technologies & Defenses)： 评估领先厂商如何保障其大语言模型与自主智能体工作流的安全，抵御对抗性攻击、提示词注入以及系统级漏洞利用。 5. 威胁向量、漏洞与数据隐私： 研究 AI 部署中出现的新型威胁向量、漏洞利用技术、模型自身缺陷，以及隐私泄露和企业数据泄露等高危风险。 6. 端侧 Agent 与终端安全 (On-Device Agent & Endpoint Security)： 探索在终端用户设备（移动端/物联网）上原生部署自主 AI 智能体所需的专属安全架构，确保严格的访问控制、端云安全通信以及稳健的威胁缓解框架。
